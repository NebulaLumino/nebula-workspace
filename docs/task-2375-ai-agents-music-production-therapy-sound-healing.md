# Task 2375: Explore AI Agents in Music Therapy, Sound Healing & Wellness Audio AI

## Overview
Music therapy is clinically proven to reduce anxiety, improve motor function, and support mental health. AI is making music therapy more accessible — generating personalized therapeutic music, monitoring physiological responses to sound, and enabling sound healing practices at scale.

## Key Applications
- **Personalized music therapy**: AI generates music sequences tailored to individual patient needs
- **Biometric-music synchronization**: AI creates music that responds in real-time to heart rate, brainwaves, or breathing
- **Sound healing sessions**: AI generates binaural beats, isochronic tones, and ambient soundscapes for wellness
- **Music for dementia**: AI creates familiar music experiences for Alzheimer's patients
- **Motor rehabilitation**: AI-generated rhythmic auditory stimulation for gait training
- **Stress reduction audio**: AI generates personalized calming soundscapes

## Tools & Frameworks
- **Endel**: Personalized soundscapes for focus and sleep
- **Brain.fm**: AI-generated music for focus and relaxation
- **Soft слух (dementia music)**: Music therapy AI for Alzheimer's
- **Google's MusicLM**: Generating music from text prompts
- **MedRhythms (neurologic music therapy)**: AI-powered rhythmic auditory stimulation
- **Somatic AI therapy sound tools**: Emerging wellness AI startups

## Ethical Considerations
- **Evidence base**: Much of sound healing lacks rigorous clinical evidence
- **Cultural appropriation**: AI may dilute traditional sound healing practices from indigenous cultures
- **Access inequality**: AI music therapy tools are expensive and proprietary
- **Dependence**: Over-reliance on AI-generated music vs. live therapeutic relationship

## Key Players
Endel, Brain.fm, MedRhythms, Google MusicLM, academic music therapy research programs
