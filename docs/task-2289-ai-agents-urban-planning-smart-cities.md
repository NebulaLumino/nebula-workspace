# Task 2289: Explore AI Agents in Urban Planning, Smart Cities & Public Transit Optimization AI

## Overview
Cities are complex adaptive systems — and AI is increasingly managing them. From traffic signal optimization to public transit scheduling to urban development planning, AI is reshaping how cities function and who they serve.

## Key Applications
- **Traffic signal AI**: Adaptive traffic signals that respond in real-time to traffic flow (Surtrac system reduced travel times 25%)
- **Public transit optimization**: AI optimizes bus routes, schedules, and real-time dispatch
- **Urban development modeling**: AI simulates the impact of zoning changes, new developments, and infrastructure investments
- **Street-level sensing**: AI processes camera and sensor data for pedestrian counts, air quality, and noise
- **Parking optimization**: AI directs drivers to available parking, reducing cruising for parking
- **Permit and zoning AI**: AI processes building permits and zoning variance requests
- **Equity analysis**: AI identifies which neighborhoods lack services and prioritizes investment

## Tools & Frameworks
- **Surtrac (Rapid Flow Technologies)**: Adaptive traffic signal AI (Pittsburgh deployed)
- **Via Transportation**: Transit optimization AI
- **ShotSpotter**: Acoustic AI for gunshot detection and urban safety
- **StreetLight Data**: Transportation analytics from mobile data
- **City of Amsterdam Smart City initiatives**: Integrated city AI systems
- **Singapore Smart Nation**: City-scale AI integration

## Ethical Considerations
- **Surveillance creep**: Smart city sensors become surveillance infrastructure
- **Algorithmic zoning**: AI planning recommendations may encode existing inequalities
- **Digital divide in transit**: Benefits may accrue to wealthy, connected neighborhoods
- **Privacy of movement data**: Aggregated pedestrian/vehicle data is still personal data
- **Civic participation**: AI urban planning may reduce democratic participation in decisions

## Key Players
Surtrac/Rapid Flow, Via Transportation, StreetLight, ShotSpotter, StreetLight Data, city governments of Singapore, Amsterdam, Barcelona
