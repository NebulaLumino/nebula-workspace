# Task 2200: Explore AI Agents in Sports Betting, Match-Fixing Detection & Integrity AI

## Overview
The global sports betting market is worth over $500B annually. AI is deployed on both sides — by betting firms to set odds and by sports leagues to detect match-fixing. The arms race between AI-powered betting and AI-powered integrity monitoring is intensifying.

## Key Applications
- **Oddsmaking and market analysis**: AI sets and adjusts odds faster and more accurately than human bookmakers
- **In-play betting**: AI enables microsecond-level in-game betting by processing game events instantly
- **Match-fixing detection**: AI flags unusual betting patterns, ref behavior, and game statistics that suggest manipulation
- **Player tracking for betting integrity**: AI monitors professional athletes for gambling-related rule violations
- **Fraudulent account detection**: AI identifies bonus abuse, multi-accounting, and arbitrage bettors
- **Responsible gambling**: AI detects problem gambling patterns in bettors and triggers interventions

## Tools & Frameworks
- **Sportradar**: AI-driven sports data and integrity monitoring (used by leagues worldwide)
- **Genius Sports**: Betting integrity and data rights for sports leagues
- **Betgenius / Playtech**: AI odds engines
- **STAR (Sports Trading Association)**: Integrity monitoring frameworks
- **UNODC AI**: Match-fixing detection in international sports

## Ethical Considerations
- **Conflict of interest**: Betting companies funding sports leagues creates integrity concerns
- **Privacy of athletes**: Athletes are monitored for gambling behavior — privacy vs. integrity tradeoff
- **Problem gambling amplification**: AI-optimized betting experiences worsen problem gambling addiction
- **Regulatory arbitrage**: AI betting operations move to least-regulated jurisdictions
- **Fixing incentives**: AI-detectable match-fixing patterns may push fixers to harder-to-detect methods
- **Youth exposure**: Mobile betting + AI personalization is reaching increasingly young audiences
