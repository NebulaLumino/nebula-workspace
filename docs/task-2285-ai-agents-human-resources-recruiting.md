# Task 2285: Explore AI Agents in Human Resources, Recruiting & Talent Intelligence AI

## Overview
HR is one of the most AI-intensive business functions — from resume screening and candidate matching to performance management and employee retention prediction. AI promises efficiency gains but raises discrimination, surveillance, and labor rights concerns.

## Key Applications
- **Resume screening**: AI filters resumes before human review, dramatically reducing time-to-screen
- **Candidate matching**: AI matches candidates to jobs based on skills, potential, and culture fit
- **Interview AI**: AI-assisted structured interviews, transcription, and analysis
- **Employee retention prediction**: AI flags flight risk employees before they quit
- **Skills inference**: AI infers skills from work history and certifications even when not explicitly listed
- **Performance management**: AI assists (or automates) performance reviews and calibration
- **HR chatbot**: AI answers employee HR policy and benefits questions 24/7

## Tools & Frameworks
- **HireVue**: AI video interview analysis
- **Pymetrics**: Neuroscience-based AI assessments
- **Eightfold.ai**: Talent intelligence platform
- **Workday (AI features)**: Enterprise HR AI
- **Greenhouse (AI features)**: Recruiting platform AI
- **Lattice / 15Five**: Performance management AI
- **Eightfold (talent platform)**: Skills ontology and talent matching

## Ethical Considerations
- **Resume screening bias**: AI trained on historical hiring perpetuates demographic biases
- **Algorithmic audits**: EEOC and EU AI Act are beginning to regulate AI hiring tools
- **Employee surveillance**: AI monitoring employee communications, keystrokes, and productivity raises labor rights concerns
- **Skills gaps and false positives**: AI may screen out great candidates who don't match traditional patterns
- **Candidate deception**: Candidates increasingly use AI to optimize for AI resume screening

## Key Players
HireVue, Pymetrics, Eightfold.ai, Workday, Greenhouse, Lattice, AllyO (HR chatbot)
