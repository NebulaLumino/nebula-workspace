# Task 2319: Explore AI Agents in Addiction Recovery, Peer Support & Sobriety Companion AI

## Overview
Addiction recovery is a lifelong process that happens mostly outside clinical settings. AI companions are emerging as 24/7 peer support — available at 3am when cravings hit, something no sponsor or therapist can provide. The evidence base is young but growing.

## Key Applications
- **Sobriety companion chatbots**: AI 24/7 peer support apps (I Am Sober, Sober Grid, Looped)
- **Craving prediction and interruption**: AI identifies patterns that trigger cravings and sends interventions before they peak
- **Meeting finder and accountability**: AI matches people to AA/NA/SMART meetings based on location, time, and modality
- **Trigger detection from phone usage**: AI detects emotional states from screen and app usage patterns that predict relapse risk
- **Peer support matching**: AI matches people in recovery with compatible peers based on history and needs
- **Post-acute withdrawal symptom support**: AI guides people through PAWS (post-acute withdrawal syndrome) which is often poorly clinically managed

## Tools & Frameworks
- **I Am Sober**: Daily pledge, tracking, and community AI features
- **Sober Grid**: Peer recovery support network with AI matching
- **Looped (AI-powered AA)**: AI-facilitated AA meeting facilitation
- **WeConnect (recovery)**: Recovery community management
- **DynamiCare (incentive-based)**: AI + incentives for recovery
- **CHESS (Health)**: Computer-based alcohol recovery support

## Ethical Considerations
- **Not a substitute for clinical care**: AI peer support should not replace therapy or medical treatment
- **Crisis response**: AI must recognize when people are in medical crisis and immediately escalate
- **Privacy in recovery**: Being identified as a person in recovery has social and sometimes legal consequences
- **Anonymity online**: NA and AA traditions of anonymity clash with AI data collection
- **Addiction to AI**: Could people become dependent on AI companions in unhealthy ways?

## Key Players
I Am Sober, Sober Grid, Looped, WeConnect, DynamiCare, CHESS Health
