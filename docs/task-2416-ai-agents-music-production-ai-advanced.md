# Task 2416: Explore AI Agents in Music Composition, Genre Transfer & AI Band Generator AI

## Overview
Beyond generating songs, AI is now creating band arrangements, transferring musical styles, and enabling anyone to compose orchestral music — all from text prompts. The implications for professional musicians and music education are profound.

## Key Applications
- **Full band arrangement generation**: AI generates drums, bass, guitar, keys, and vocals for a song from a prompt
- **Style transfer for music**: Transform a song from pop to jazz, classical to EDM
- **AI jam sessions**: Real-time AI accompaniment that responds to human playing
- **Sheet music generation**: AI converts audio or descriptions into printable sheet music
- **Remix generation**: AI generates legal remixes by recombining musical elements
- **Binaural beat generator**: AI generates therapeutic audio frequencies

## Tools & Frameworks
- **Suno AI**: Full song generation with vocals, instruments, and production
- **Udio**: High-quality AI music generation
- **AIVA**: Classical and cinematic music composition AI
- **LANDR**: AI mastering and distribution
- **Boomy**: AI music generation for social media
- **Google MusicLM**: Research model for long-form coherent music generation

## Ethical Considerations
- **Sampling without consent**: AI music models trained on copyrighted music without clearance
- **Musician livelihoods**: Session musicians, arrangers, and composers face unprecedented threat
- **Music homogenization**: AI optimization for broad appeal may flatten musical diversity
- **Authorship and royalties**: When AI generates a hit song, who gets paid?
- **Deepfake music**: AI cloning artists' voices for unauthorized recordings

## Key Players
Suno, Udio, AIVA, LANDR, Boomy, Google Research (MusicLM), Stability Audio
