# Task 2386: Explore AI Agents in Aging, Senior Care, Gerontechnology & Longevity AI

## Overview
Global aging is one of the most significant demographic shifts in human history. AI-powered gerontechnology is addressing the challenges of caring for aging populations — from elder care monitoring to longevity research.

## Key Applications
- **Elder care monitoring**: AI detects falls, gait changes, and health decline from sensors
- **Social companionship AI**: AI chatbots reduce loneliness for isolated elders
- **Cognitive assessment**: AI detects early dementia and cognitive decline from speech and behavior
- **Medication management**: AI reminds and monitors medication adherence
- **Longevity research**: AI analyzes biomarkers to predict biological age and disease risk
- **Caregiver support**: AI helps family caregivers manage care logistics and respite

## Tools & Frameworks
- **Care.coach**: AI companion for elderly isolated individuals
- **CarePredict**: AI wearable for senior care monitoring
- **Alexa Together (Amazon)**: Elder care AI subscription service
- **Intuition Robotics (ElliQ)**: Social robot for seniors
- **Google Health (cognitive assessment)**: AI cognitive screening tools
- **Altos Labs (Longevity)**: AI-driven longevity research (founded 2022)

## Ethical Considerations
- **Dignity and surveillance**: Constant monitoring can undermine elders' dignity and autonomy
- **Human contact replacement**: AI companionship may reduce human contact, worsening loneliness
- **Longevity inequality**: AI longevity treatments will be accessible only to the wealthy
- **Care labor**: AI doesn't solve the underlying care labor shortage
- **False independence**: AI monitoring may give families false confidence about elders' safety

## Key Players
Amazon (Alexa Together), CarePredict, Intuition Robotics, Care.coach, Altos Labs, Calico (Alphabet), human longevity startups
