# Task 2226: Explore AI Agents in Hair Care, Beauty & Cosmetics AI

**Status**: ✅ Complete

## Overview
Beauty AI spans skincare analysis, makeup try-on, cosmetic formulation, and personalized product recommendations. Computer vision + LLMs are enabling unprecedented personalization and diagnosis in the $500B+ beauty industry.

## Key Applications
- **Skin analysis AI**: Analyze photos of skin to identify concerns (acne, pigmentation, wrinkles, rosacea) and recommend products
- **Virtual makeup try-on**: AR + AI overlay for realistic lipstick, eyeshadow, foundation try-on
- **Hair analysis**: AI analyzes scalp and hair strand photos for hair loss diagnosis, product recommendations
- **Fragrance matching**: AI recommends perfumes based on olfactory preference profiles and complementary notes
- **Cosmetic formulation**: AI optimizes cosmetic formulations for texture, stability, and skin compatibility
- **Beauty routine builder**: AI generates personalized multi-step skincare routines based on skin type, concerns, and lifestyle

## Tools & Frameworks
- **Perfect Corp (YouCam)**: AR beauty try-on used by Estée Lauder, L'Oréal
- **L'Oréal (ModiFace)**: Skin AI and virtual try-on
- **Curology (dermatology AI)**: Prescription skincare with AI analysis
- **Function of Beauty**: AI-customized hair care formulations
- **Beautycounter AI**: Ingredient safety screening AI

## Ethical Considerations
- **Skin tone bias**: AI beauty tools historically performed poorly on darker skin tones
- **Body image amplification**: AI-perfect skin filters and virtual try-on may worsen body dysmorphia
- **Skin condition misdiagnosis**: AI skin analysis could delay real medical diagnosis
- **Ingredient greenwashing**: AI might be used to make misleading "clean beauty" claims

## Key Players
L'Oréal (ModiFace), Perfect Corp, Estée Lauder, Curology, Function of Beauty, Beautycounter
