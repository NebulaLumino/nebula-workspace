## AI Agents in Estate Planning & Trust Administration

Estate planning is one of the most consequential yet backlogged areas of legal practice. AI agents are now being deployed to draft trusts, model tax exposure, manage trust distributions, and help families navigate probate — reducing the cost of estate administration from tens of thousands of dollars to a fraction of that.

### The Estate Planning Workflow

Estate planning involves:
1. **Asset inventory** — Identifying all assets, liabilities, and beneficial interests
2. **Beneficiary mapping** — Understanding who inherits what under which scenario
3. **Tax modeling** — Projecting estate tax, gift tax, income tax, and generation-skipping transfer tax exposure
4. **Document drafting** — Wills, trusts, powers of attorney, healthcare directives
5. **Administration** — Managing distributions, tax filings, and trust accounting after death

AI agents are now touching every step of this workflow.

### AI Document Drafting Agents

LLM-based agents can now draft:
- **Last Will & Testaments** — With state-specific statutory requirements
- **Revocable Living Trusts** — Including pour-over provisions and successor trustee instructions
- **Irrevocable Life Insurance Trusts (ILITs)** — With Crummey notice procedures
- **Dynasty Trusts** — Multi-generational wealth preservation structures

Platforms like **Trust & Will**, **MarketWatch Tailored**, and **Rocket Lawyer** use LLM agents to generate compliant documents. More sophisticated systems integrate with tax modeling to recommend optimal trust structures.

### Trust Administration Agents

Once a trust is established, AI agents can serve as **trust Protectors** or **distribution advisors**:

- Monitor trust investments against fiduciary standards
- Model distribution scenarios (lump sum vs. staggered)
- Calculate and track GST (Generation-Skipping Transfer) tax exemption usage
- Generate trust accountings required by beneficiaries

### Tax Projection Agents

AI agents run Monte Carlo simulations on estate portfolios to project:
- Likelihood of estate tax exposure at various asset growth rates
- Optimal timing of gifts to maximize GST exemption utilization
- Comparison of trust structures (SLAT vs. IDGT vs. GRAT) for given asset profiles

### Probate Management Agents

Probate is the court-supervised process of administering a deceased person's estate. AI agents can:
- Generate the inventory of estate assets from financial account data
- Draft probate court filings (petitions, inventories, accountings)
- Identify creditor claims and manage the claims period
- Model partial distribution scenarios

### The Attorney Supervision Model

Critically, most jurisdictions require that AI-generated estate planning documents be reviewed by a licensed attorney. AI agents are positioned as **attorney assistants** — doing the drafting, research, and calculation work while the attorney applies professional judgment to client-specific nuances.

### Ethical Considerations

| Concern | Issue |
|---------|-------|
| Client Data | Estate data is extremely sensitive; data retention policies matter |
| Unbundled Legal Services | AI drafting without attorney review may constitute unauthorized practice of law |
| Capacity Issues | AI must recognize signs of diminished capacity in testators |
| Conflict of Interest | AI managing both sides of a transaction needs conflict checks |

Estate planning AI agents represent a major opportunity to democratize access to wealth preservation — but the human element of understanding a family's values and dynamics remains difficult to automate fully.
