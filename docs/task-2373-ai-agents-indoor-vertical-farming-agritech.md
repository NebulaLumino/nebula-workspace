# Task 2373: Explore AI Agents in Indoor Farming, Controlled Environment Agriculture & Agritech AI

## Overview
Controlled Environment Agriculture (CEA) — growing crops indoors under artificial light — is one of the most technically sophisticated applications of AI. From LED light recipe optimization to robotic harvesting, AI is making indoor farming increasingly economically viable.

## Key Applications
- **LED spectrum optimization**: AI tunes light recipes (red/blue/far-red ratios) for each crop stage
- **Climate automation**: Real-time control of temperature, humidity, CO2, and airflow
- **Yield prediction**: AI predicts harvest timing and quantity from sensor data
- **Robotic harvesting**: AI-guided robotic arms harvest delicate crops (lettuce, strawberries)
- **Disease detection**: Computer vision detects plant disease before it spreads
- **Water and nutrient optimization**: AI precisely delivers water and nutrients ( aeroponics/hydroponics)

## Tools & Frameworks
- **Bowery Farming OS**: End-to-end indoor farming AI platform
- **AppHarvest**: AI-guided greenhouse growing
- **Farming Revolution**: AI-controlled indoor farming systems
- **Illumedata**: AI for vertical farming lighting
- **Iron Ox**: Fully autonomous greenhouse robots
- **Agritecture (consulting)**: CEA planning with AI tools

## Ethical Considerations
- **Energy sustainability**: Indoor farms use enormous electricity — benefits require renewable energy
- **Local food vs. industrial scale**: Does indoor farming really reduce environmental impact?
- **Labor displacement**: Automated indoor farms eliminate agricultural jobs
- **Access and pricing**: Indoor farm produce is premium-priced — who benefits?
