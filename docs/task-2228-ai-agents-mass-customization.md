# Task 2228: Explore AI Agents in Mass Customization, Made-to-Order Fashion & 3D Body Scanning

**Status**: ✅ Complete

## Overview
Mass customization — making every garment to an individual's exact measurements — is finally becoming economically viable. AI-powered 3D body scanning, automated pattern making, and on-demand production are enabling a new model for fashion.

## Key Applications
- **3D body scanning**: AI extracts accurate body measurements from photos or smartphone depth sensors
- **Automated pattern making**: AI generates custom patterns from individual measurements without manual drafting
- **Virtual fit simulation**: AI predicts how a garment will fit a specific body before production
- **Size prediction from photos**: AI estimates measurements from photos when 3D scan unavailable
- **Fit feedback loop**: AI analyzes return data to improve pattern grading over time
- **Made-to-order manufacturing**: AI coordinates on-demand production to eliminate inventory

## Tools & Frameworks
- **TrueGathers**: 3D body scanning for fashion
- **3DLook**: AI body measurement from photos
- **Unmade**: AI-driven mass customization platform for fashion brands
- **Browzwear / CLO3D**: Virtual fit and pattern making
- **Alvanon**: 3D body scan database for apparel industry

## Ethical Considerations
- **Body image and measurement anxiety**: Having precise measurements may increase body dissatisfaction
- **Privacy of body data**: Detailed body scans are extremely sensitive data
- **Fit standard homogenization**: AI fit models may push toward a narrow definition of "normal" bodies
- **Environmental benefit vs. consumption**: Made-to-order could reduce waste but enable more total consumption

## Key Players
3DLook, TrueGathers, Unmade, Browzwear, Alvanon, Size-ID (3D printing startups)
