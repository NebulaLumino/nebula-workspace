## App Concept

Generate coupon codes and discount campaign strategies for e-commerce marketing. Input: campaign goal (clearance, new customer acquisition, loyalty, holiday), discount type (%, $ off, BOGO, free shipping), budget, target customer segment, expiration date. Output: campaign copy, suggested codes, urgency messaging, and channel recommendations.
