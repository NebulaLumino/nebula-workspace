## App Concept

Generate affiliate partner performance reports and optimization recommendations. Input: affiliate name, affiliate type (blogger, coupon site, comparison site), traffic volume estimate, conversion metrics (clicks, leads, sales, revenue), commission paid, affiliate since date. Output: structured affiliate performance report with traffic-to-conversion funnel, ROI calculation, commission efficiency score, top-performing content types, seasonal trends, and personalized optimization tips for the affiliate partner.
