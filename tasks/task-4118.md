## Description
An AI-powered tool that creates graded exposure hierarchies for social anxiety. Users identify feared social situations, and the AI builds a personalized exposure ladder from least to most anxiety-provoking, with specific practice scenarios, coping strategies, and progress tracking.

## Technical
- **Framework:** Next.js 15 (App Router)
- **AI SDK:** OpenAI (DeepSeek compatible)
- **Deployment:** Vercel

## Workflow
1. User lists feared social situations and rates anxiety levels
2. AI organizes situations into a graded exposure hierarchy
3. AI generates specific, actionable practice scenarios for each step
4. User logs practice attempts and anxiety ratings
5. AI provides encouragement and next-step recommendations
