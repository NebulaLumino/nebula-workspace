## App Concept

Generate helpful Quora answers that drive traffic. Input: question text, topic area, your expertise level, link to include (product/resource), answer length preference, tone (authoritative, conversational, beginner-friendly). Output: complete Quora answer with hook opening, well-structured body, sourced references, and subtle CTA. Includes answer formatting tips (bold, bullets, quotes) for Quora's algorithm.
