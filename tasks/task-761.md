**Status:** ✅ Complete

## App Concept

Generate structured SWOT analysis documents. Input: company/product name, industry, key competitors, internal capabilities, external market trends. Output: complete SWOT matrix with S/W/O/T quadrants, supporting evidence for each point, strategic implications per quadrant, and recommended actions.
