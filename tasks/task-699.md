## App Concept

Generate newsworthy press releases from event or announcement details. Input: announcement type (product launch, funding, partnership, award, event, executive hire), company name, key facts, quote from spokesperson, supporting data, contact name/email. Output: AP-style press release with compelling headline, subheadline, dateline, lede paragraph, boilerplate, ### ending, and suggested distribution channels.
