## App Concept

Generate comprehensive Terms of Service documents for websites and apps. Input: company name, website/app type, business model (SaaS, marketplace, content, e-commerce), data collected, user-generated content features, payment processing. Output: complete ToS document with sections on account terms, acceptable use, intellectual property, payment terms, disclaimer of warranties, limitation of liability, termination clauses, and governing law. Includes a plain-English summary version.

- DeepSeek API (OpenAI-compatible)
