# Task 763: AI Competitive Landscape Matrix Generator

**Status:** ✅ Complete
**Type:** App Generator — Strategy
**Priority:** HIGH
**Cycle:** 27 (761-790)

## App Concept

Generate visual competitive positioning matrices. Input: your product, 3-6 competitors, positioning axes (e.g., price vs. features, simplicity vs. power), target market segment. Output: 2x2 matrix with competitor positions plotted, your product highlighted, strategic positioning recommendations, and whitespace opportunities.

## Tech Stack
- Next.js 16 (App Router), Tailwind CSS, DeepSeek API, TypeScript
- GitHub repo: `ai-competitive-landscape-matrix`
