## App Concept

Generate lead magnet ideas and full asset outlines for content upgrades. Input: blog post title or product category, target audience persona, content format preference (checklist, template, toolkit, cheatsheet, mini-course), SEO search intent, competitor lead magnets observed. Output: lead magnet concept with title, cover/copy concept, full table of contents/outline, delivery format recommendation, upsell path suggestion, and promotional email copy to drive signups.
