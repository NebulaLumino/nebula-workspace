## App Concept
Generate customer success stories and narratives from data points. Input: customer company, industry, problem they faced, solution implemented, measurable outcome (with numbers), customer quote. Output: narrative success story with headline, challenge/solution/results structure, key metrics highlighted, customer bio, and call-to-action.
