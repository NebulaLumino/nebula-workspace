## App Concept

Generate structured sprint retrospective facilitation guides. Input: sprint number, sprint goal, team size, what went well, what didn't go well, immediate blockages experienced, team dynamics notes. Output: complete retrospective facilitation guide with set-the-stage prompts, activities (start/stop/continue, Mad/Sad/Glad, 4Ls), action items with owners and due dates, sprint health radar chart data, and team norms recommendations. Includes facilitator tips per activity.
