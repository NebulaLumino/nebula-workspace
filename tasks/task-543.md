## App Concept

Generate comprehensive project charters from a one-sentence idea. Input: project name, objective, stakeholders, timeline rough estimate, budget range, constraints. Output: full project charter with SMART goals, success metrics, risk register, RACI matrix, milestone timeline, and communication plan. Exportable as PDF.
