## App Concept

Generate a GDPR/CCPA-compliant cookie consent banner with granular controls. Input: website name, cookie categories used (essential, analytics, marketing, functional), privacy policy URL, and brand color. Output: copy + suggested layout for a non-annoying, legally-sound cookie banner with "Accept All," "Reject All," and "Manage Preferences" options, plus a preferences modal.
