# Task 885: AI Survey Questions Generator

**Status:** ✅ Complete
**Type:** App Generator — Research
**Priority:** HIGH
**Cycle:** 30 (871-900)

## App Concept
Generate survey question sets for research projects. Input: survey topic, target respondents, survey goal (product feedback, brand research, market sizing), length preference. Output: complete survey with question bank organized by section, question types (Likert, multiple choice, open text), and analysis guide.

## Tech Stack
- Next.js 16 (App Router), Tailwind CSS, DeepSeek API, TypeScript
- GitHub repo: `ai-survey-questions`
