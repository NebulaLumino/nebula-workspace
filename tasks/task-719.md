## App Concept

Generate Objectives and Key Results (OKRs) for teams and individuals. Input: team/individual name, quarter (Q1-Q4), company-level OKRs context, focus areas (3-5), previous quarter performance context. Output: 3-5 objectives with 2-4 key results per objective, each KR with a measurable metric and baseline/target, confidence level rating (1-10) per KR, and suggested initiatives. Includes department-specific OKR examples.
