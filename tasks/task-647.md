## App Concept

Generate interview questions for developing customer personas. Input: persona role/title, industry, company size, product/service being validated, research goals (understand pain points, buying process, decision makers, etc.), interview format (30-min, 60-min, quantitative survey). Output: structured interview guide with opening questions, probing questions grouped by theme, follow-up prompts, closing questions, and optional survey questions for quantitative validation.
