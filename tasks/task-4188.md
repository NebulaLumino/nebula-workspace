`ai-partnership-summary`

## Description
Build an AI-powered document analysis tool that uploads partnership agreements (NDAs, MSAs, JV agreements) and auto-extracts key terms, obligations, financials, termination clauses, and risk flags into a structured summary.

## Requirements
- PDF/DOCX upload with OCR fallback
- AI extraction of key clauses: Parties, Term, Jurisdiction, IP, Termination, Liability, Confidentiality, Exclusivity, Revenue Share, ROFR
- Side-by-side comparison of two agreement versions
- Risk flagging (unusual termination windows, unlimited liability, auto-renewal traps)
- Obligation calendar (deliverables, payments, reviews)
- Plain-English summary for non-lawyers
- "Ask about this agreement" Q&A interface
- Risk score and red flag index
- Negotiation history tracking
