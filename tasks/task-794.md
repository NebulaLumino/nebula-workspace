## App Concept
Generate trade show and conference booth strategies. Input: event name, target audience, product/service, booth size, team size, budget tier. Output: booth strategy with staffing schedule, lead capture forms, demo flow, giveaways recommendations, follow-up sequence, and pre/post-event outreach plan.
