**Status:** ✅ Complete
**Type:** App Generator — Marketing/Analytics
**Priority:** HIGH

## App Concept
Generate conversion funnel analysis reports. Input: funnel stages, traffic at each stage, conversion rates, drop-off points, time to convert. Output: funnel analysis with drop-off visualization, root cause hypotheses, 5 improvement recommendations, and projected impact of fixing each drop-off point.
