## Description
An AI-powered tool that generates systematic desensitization scripts for specific phobias. Users identify their fear, and the AI creates a graduated exposure hierarchy with relaxation scripts for each step, following evidence-based phobia treatment protocols.

## Technical
- **Framework:** Next.js 15 (App Router)
- **AI SDK:** OpenAI (DeepSeek compatible)
- **Deployment:** Vercel

## Workflow
1. User identifies a specific phobia and rates fear levels
2. AI creates a graded exposure hierarchy from least to most frightening
3. AI generates relaxation and coping scripts for each hierarchy level
4. User practices exposure steps with guided scripts
5. Progress tracking with fear ratings at each level
