## Description
Build an AI-powered brand monitoring tool that tracks mentions across social media, news, forums, and review sites, performs sentiment analysis, identifies emerging trends, and generates alerts.

## Requirements
- Multi-platform mention tracking (Twitter/X, Reddit, News, Reviews)
- Sentiment analysis (positive, negative, neutral, nuanced)
- Theme/topic clustering of mentions
- Share of Voice vs competitors
- Influence scoring for mention authors
- Crisis detection (sudden spike in negative mentions)
- Trending topic identification
- Weekly/monthly brand health reports
- Alert rules (threshold-based, keyword-based)
- Competitive mention benchmarking
