## App Concept

Generate official rules and legal disclosures for giveaways, sweepstakes, and contest promotions. Input: brand name, promotion type (giveaway, UGC contest, sweepstakes), prize value, entry mechanism, platform (Instagram, TikTok, email, website), eligibility region. Output: complete official rules document, eligibility terms, entry instructions, winner selection method, prize description, liability waivers, and social media disclosure language.
