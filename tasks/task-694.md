## App Concept

Generate full YouTube video scripts from a topic or outline. Input: video topic/title, video format (tutorial, vlog, review, interview, commentary), target audience, video length, channel style reference, SEO keyword focus. Output: complete video script with cold open hook, timestamped outline, each section with narration + B-roll suggestions, CTAs at natural breaks, end screen script, and estimated watch time per segment.

- DeepSeek API (OpenAI-compatible)
- TypeScript
