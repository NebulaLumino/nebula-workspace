## Research Findings — AI Customer Support Automation 2026

### AI Support Platforms

| Platform | Type | What It Does |
|---|---|---|
| **Intercom Fin** | AI chatbot | Resolves support tickets autonomously |
| **Forethought** | Ticket routing | AI categorizes and assigns tickets |
| **Zendesk AI** | Suite | AI agent + help center + analytics |
| **Freshdesk Freddy** | AI agent | Customer support automation |
| **Salesforce Einstein** | CRM AI | Case routing + suggestions |
| **Claude (Anthropic)** | API | Build custom support AI |

### How It Works

1. **Incoming ticket** → AI reads and classifies intent
2. **Routing** → AI assigns to right team/category
3. **First response** → AI drafts reply (human approves or auto-sends)
4. **Resolution** → AI suggests knowledge base articles
5. **Escalation** → AI detects frustration and escalates to human

### Sentiment Analysis

AI detects customer emotion from text:
- **Frustrated**: escalation flag
- **Satisfied**: opportunity for NPS survey
- **Confused**: proactive help offer
- **Angry**: priority flag + supervisor alert

### Resolution Rates

| Platform | Auto-resolution |
|---|---|
| Fin (Intercom) | ~50% of conversations |
| Zendesk AI | ~40% |
| Custom (Claude) | Varies widely |

### Key Challenges

- **Accuracy**: Wrong answers damage trust
- **Handoffs**: Seamless human-AI transitions are hard
- **Context**: Bot forgets conversation history
- **Sensitive issues**: Billing, legal, account deletion need human review
