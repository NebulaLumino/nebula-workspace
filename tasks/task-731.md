## App Concept

Generate memorable brand taglines and slogans. Input: brand name, industry, key brand personality traits, target audience, competitors' taglines to differentiate from, tone preference. Output: 10 tagline variations across different styles (aspirational, provocative, descriptive, emotional, witty), scoring each for memorability, differentiation, and brand fit, plus a shortlist of top 3 with rationale.
