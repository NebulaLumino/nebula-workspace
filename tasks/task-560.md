## App Concept

Generate operational runbooks for incident response and procedures. Input: system name, failure mode or procedure type, stakeholders, escalation contacts, previous incident notes. Output: step-by-step runbook with pre-checks, diagnosis steps, resolution steps, rollback procedures, post-incident checklist, and communication templates. Designed for on-call engineers.
