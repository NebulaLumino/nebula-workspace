## App Concept

Generate supplier scorecards and performance reports for procurement teams. Input: supplier name, product categories, delivery metrics, quality scores, cost data, communication rating. Output: weighted scorecard with KPI breakdown, grade/rating, improvement recommendations, and comparison against industry benchmarks.
