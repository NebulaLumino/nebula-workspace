## App Concept

Generate attractive careers pages that attract top talent. Input: company name, culture description, benefits offered, remote/hybrid policy, open roles list (or general culture description), team size, DEI commitments. Output: compelling careers page with company culture overview, employee value proposition, benefits highlights (with icons), remote/hybrid policy explanation, interview process description, and a general "join our team" CTA. Multiple tone variations (fun, serious, family-like, high-performance).

## Tech Stack

- Next.js 16 (App Router)
- Tailwind CSS
- DeepSeek API (OpenAI-compatible)
- TypeScript
