**Status:** ✅ Complete
**Type:** App Generator — Marketing
**Priority:** HIGH

## App Concept
Generate complete landing page copy from a campaign brief. Input: campaign goal, product/service, target audience, primary CTA, supporting proof points, visual direction. Output: full landing page copy including hero headline + subheadline, feature sections, social proof section, FAQ, and CTA copy. Multiple tone variations.
