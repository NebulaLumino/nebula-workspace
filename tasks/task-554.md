## App Concept

Analyze support tickets to find deflection opportunities. Input: list of support tickets with categories, resolution status, deflection potential rating, related documentation gaps. Output: deflection opportunity report ranked by frequency and effort, article recommendations for top deflectable categories, ROI estimate for self-service improvement, and roadmap suggestions for help center expansion.
