## App Concept

Analyze NPS survey responses and generate actionable reports. Input: NPS score, respondent count, promoter/passive/detractor breakdown, verbatim comments, time period, customer segments. Output: NPS analysis report with score interpretation, key theme extraction from comments, segment breakdown, response rate analysis, and prioritized recommendations to improve NPS.
