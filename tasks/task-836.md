## App Concept
Generate fundraising outreach emails and pitch materials. Input: investor type (VC, angel, family office), company stage, funding round, amount raised, use of funds, founder story. Output: fundraising email sequence with intro email, follow-up cadence, pitch deck talking points, and due diligence FAQ responses.
