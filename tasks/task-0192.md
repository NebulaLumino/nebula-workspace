## Research Findings — AI for Personal Productivity 2026

### Email AI

**Superhuman AI (2024)**
- Keyboard-first email client with AI built in
- "Ask AI" button: summarize thread, draft reply, find something
- Integrates with Gmail, Outlook
- Best for: power users who want speed

**Shortwave**
- Gmail client with AI built in
- Auto-summarizes emails, extracts action items
- "Smart folders" auto-categorize by urgency/relevance
- Best for: heavy Gmail users who get overwhelmed

**Heated** (new in 2026)
- AI email writing coach — suggests responses, learns your voice
- Actually teaches you to write better emails

### Calendar AI

**Clockwise**
- AI optimizes your calendar to create focus time
- Analyzes meeting patterns → finds deep work blocks
- Team-level coordination (meeting travel time, etc.)

**Reclaim.ai**
- Automatically schedules habits (exercise, lunch breaks)
- Defends calendar time for priorities
- Best for: async teams, busy professionals

### Second Brain / Knowledge

**Notion AI**
- Q&A over your workspace
- Auto-summarize, brainstorm, draft

**Obsidian + AI plugins**
- Local vault with local AI (Ollama)
- Complete privacy, offline-first

**Mem.ai**
- "Memory for your mind" — captures what you read, write, think
- AI retrieves and connects across your knowledge base

### Meeting Notes AI

**Fireflies.ai**
- Joins calls, transcribes, extracts action items
- Searchable meeting archive
- Integrates with CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce)

**Otter.ai**
- Real-time transcription + summary
- Speaker identification

**Fathom Video**
- Records video + transcription + highlights (best-in-class 2026)
