## App Concept
Generate subscription box themes and product curation strategies. Input: category (beauty, food, fitness, pets), price tier ($20/$50/$100), target demographic, number of products per box, seasonality. Output: box theme concepts, product category mix, sample product suggestions, unboxing experience design, and supplier sourcing strategy.
