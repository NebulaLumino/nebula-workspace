## App Concept

Generate optimized hashtag strategies for social posts. Input: post content/topic, platform (Instagram, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, TikTok), post goal (reach, engagement, niche community), competitor hashtags used, number of hashtags desired. Output: hashtag sets organized by category (trending, niche, branded), estimated reach per hashtag, optimal number recommendations, and banned/flagged hashtag warnings.
