# Task 373: AI Proposal Executive Summary Generator

**Status:** ⏳ Pending  
**Type:** App Generator — Sales  
**Priority:** MEDIUM  

## Context
The executive summary section of a sales proposal — the make-or-break section that executives read first.

## Spec
- **Input:** Client name, their main challenge, proposed solution, expected outcomes, investment amount, timeline
- **Output:** Compelling executive summary with problem statement, solution overview, business case, and call to action
- **Length:** 200-400 words, board-ready tone

- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS
- OpenAI SDK
