## Description

Next.js app that generates comprehensive space law and Outer Space Treaty compliance reports. Includes:
- Outer Space Treaty (1967) article-by-article analysis
- National space legislation (US, EU, Luxembourg, UAE, Japan, China)
- Space resource rights (US CSLCA, Artemis Accords Section 10)
- FAA launch licensing and ITU coordination compliance
- Liability framework and dispute resolution mechanisms
