## Overview
How do we measure AI capability? The evolving landscape of AI benchmarks and evaluation methodologies.

## Research Questions
- Why is AI evaluation hard? The "impossible" benchmarking problem
- Key benchmarks: MMLU, HumanEval, GSM8K, BIG-Bench, HELM, SWE-bench
- Do benchmarks correlate with real-world performance?
- The Goodhart's Law problem: when a measure becomes a target, it ceases to be a good measure
- Evals: how companies build custom evaluation suites
- Emerging: agentic benchmarks, multimodal benchmarks, long-context benchmarks
- Who should set AI benchmarks? Government? Industry? Academia?
