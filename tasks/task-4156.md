## Description
Build an AI-powered business valuation and fairness opinion generator. Input business financial data (revenue, EBITDA, assets) and receive a comprehensive valuation using DCF, comparable company analysis (CCA), precedent transactions, and AI-adjusted risk premiums. Generates professional fairness opinion documents for M&A, litigation, and succession planning.

## Requirements
- DCF (Discounted Cash Flow) model builder
- Comparable company analysis (public comps)
- Precedent transaction analysis
- Revenue multiple valuation
- EBITDA multiple valuation
- AI risk-adjusted discount rate
- Goodwill impairment testing
- Fairness opinion letter generator
- M&A synergy estimator
- PDF/Excel export of valuation model
