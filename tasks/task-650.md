## App Concept

Generate customer journey maps from business descriptions. Input: business type, target customer persona, products/services offered, primary conversion goal, key touchpoints (website, app, store, support, social), existing journey insights. Output: comprehensive customer journey map with stages (awareness, consideration, purchase, retention, advocacy), actions, thoughts, emotions, and opportunities at each stage, plus pain points, moment-of-truth analysis, and improvement recommendations.
