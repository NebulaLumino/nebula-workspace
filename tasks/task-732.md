## App Concept

Generate creative and brandable product names. Input: product category, key features, target demographic, naming constraints (available domain, trademark-free, prefix/suffix requirements), tone (professional, playful, technical, luxury). Output: 20+ product name options with availability indicators (domain, trademark), pronunciation guide, meaning/origin explanation, and domain availability check suggestions.
