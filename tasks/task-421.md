## App Concept

Generate personalized retention strategies for at-risk customers. Input: churn signals, customer health data, contract details. Output: tailored retention playbook with specific offers, escalation paths, and success metrics.

## Features

- Churn risk input form (health score, engagement metrics, contract data)
- AI generates retention strategy: engagement tactics, discount offers, escalation triggers
- Segment by churn risk level (high/medium/low)
- Export to PDF, copy-to-clipboard
