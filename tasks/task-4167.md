## Description
Comprehensive research document exploring how AI is transforming microfinance and financial inclusion. Covers AI-powered micro-lending (Kiva, Grameen-style apps), credit building for the unbanked using alternative data, digital wallet analytics, and the ethical considerations of AI in serving low-income populations.
