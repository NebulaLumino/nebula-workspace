## App Concept

Generate feature comparison tables for product marketing. Input: your product name, 2-5 competitor products, comparison categories (features, pricing, support), your key differentiators. Output: formatted comparison table with checkmarks/X for feature availability, pricing tier comparison, pros/cons per competitor, and suggested "winner" callouts per category. Markdown and HTML output formats.
