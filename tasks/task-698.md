## App Concept

Generate professional media kits for brands and creators. Input: brand/creator name, industry/niche, key statistics (audience size, engagement rate, follower demographics), notable press mentions, brand collaborations, contact information. Output: complete media kit with brand overview section, audience demographics, statistics highlight cards, platform breakdowns, sponsored content pricing tiers, case studies summary, press mentions, and brand assets description.
