## App Concept

Generate regulatory compliance checklists for specific frameworks. Input: industry (healthcare, fintech, e-commerce, SaaS), applicable regulations (HIPAA, SOC2, GDPR, PCI-DSS, ISO 27001), company size, data handled. Output: structured checklist with required controls, evidence collection items, pass/fail criteria, and remediation priority levels.
