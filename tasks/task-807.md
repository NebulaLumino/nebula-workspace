# Task 807: AI Retargeting Ad Strategy Generator

**Status:** ✅ Complete
**Type:** App Generator — Advertising
**Priority:** MEDIUM

## App Concept
Generate retargeting ad strategies and creative concepts. Input: website/platform (Meta, Google, LinkedIn), audience segment (visited page, abandoned cart, video viewers, past purchasers), offer or message. Output: retargeting strategy with audience size estimates, creative concept descriptions, bid strategy, frequency recommendations, and sequential ad storytelling concepts.
