## Description
Build an AI-powered cryptocurrency news sentiment and market impact analyzer. Scrapes news from major crypto outlets, social media (Twitter/X, Reddit), and on-chain signals, then uses AI to classify sentiment, measure influence scores, and predict short-term price impact for specific tokens and the broader market.

## Requirements
- Multi-source news aggregation (CoinDesk, The Block, Cointelegraph)
- Twitter/X sentiment analysis
- Reddit (r/cryptocurrency, r/bitcoin) trend detection
- On-chain whale activity correlation
- AI sentiment scoring (bullish/bearish/neutral)
- Market impact prediction model
- Token-specific sentiment dashboard
- Daily/weekly sentiment reports
- Alert for sudden sentiment shifts
