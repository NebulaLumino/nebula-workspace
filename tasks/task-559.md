## App Concept

Generate Entity-Relationship diagrams from text descriptions. Input: application domain, list of entities, their attributes, and relationships (one-to-many, many-to-many, inheritance). Output: visual ER diagram (ASCII/text-based), relationship summary table, cardinality notation explanation, and normalization analysis with suggested improvements.
