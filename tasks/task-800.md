# Task 800: AI Brand Guidelines Generator

**Status:** ✅ Complete
**Type:** App Generator — Brand/Design
**Priority:** HIGH
**Cycle:** 28 (791-820)

## App Concept
Generate comprehensive brand style guides from brand inputs. Input: brand name, personality traits, target audience, competitive landscape, primary color preference, tone guidelines. Output: brand guidelines document with logo usage rules, color palette (hex codes), typography scale, voice and tone dos/don'ts, photography style, and social media formatting rules.
