## App Concept

Paste a raw meeting transcript and get a structured summary in seconds. Input: transcript text (or upload .txt/.vtt). Output: executive summary, key decisions made, action items with owners and due dates, questions raised, and a sentiment/tone readout. Great for async team alignment after long calls.
