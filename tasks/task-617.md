## App Concept

Generate hashtag strategies and sets for social media posts. Input: post topic/caption, platform (Instagram, TikTok, X/Twitter, LinkedIn), post goal (reach, engagement, niche), competitor hashtags. Output: 20-30 relevant hashtags grouped by category (trending, niche, branded), hashtag performance tips, banned/restricted hashtag warnings, and platform-specific best practices.
