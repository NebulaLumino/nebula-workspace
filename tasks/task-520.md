## App Concept

Build detailed customer personas from your product description and market context. Input: product/service description, target market, existing customer data or notes. Output: 2-3 persona profiles with name, photo placeholder, demographics, goals, pain points, preferred channels, buying triggers, and sample objections with rebuttals.
