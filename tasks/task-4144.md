## Description
Build an AI-powered cryptocurrency tax optimization tool. Connects to exchanges and wallets via API, identifies tax loss harvesting opportunities, simulates wash sale rules across jurisdictions, generates IRS-compatible tax reports (Form 8949, Schedule D), and recommends optimal harvesting strategies to minimize tax liability.

## Requirements
- Multi-exchange wallet aggregation
- Cost basis calculation (FIFO, LIFO, HIFO, Specific ID)
- Wash sale rule detection (US & international)
- Tax loss harvesting opportunity scanner
- AI tax strategy advisor
- Capital gains/losses report generator
- IRS Form 8949 export
- Tax year comparison tool
