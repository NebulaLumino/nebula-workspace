## Executive Summary

AI agents are transforming insurance pricing (personalized telematics-based risk scoring), claims automation (instant FNOL, fraud detection, computer vision damage assessment), underwriting (document intelligence, cyber risk AI), and actuarial reserving (ML models improving accuracy 8-15% for long-tail lines). Lemonade processes 30% of claims instantly. Allstate underwrites 40% of small commercial accounts with AI. Telematics UBI is expanding beyond auto to home, life, and business insurance. Key challenges: regulatory pricing fairness (EU AI Act high-risk classification), explainability requirements for rate filings, data privacy, and actuarial profession reskilling. The field is moving toward real-time continuous pricing, embedded insurance AI at point of transaction, and AI actuarial copilots increasing productivity 30-50%.

## Key Concepts

- Telematics UBI (usage-based insurance) for auto/home/life
- GLMs vs. ML for actuarial pricing (interpretability tradeoff)
- AI FNOL (First Notice of Loss) triaging and instant claims
- Computer vision for damage estimation (87% accuracy within $500)
- Cyber insurance AI underwriting (accumulation risk modeling)
- ML reserving (gradient boosting, 8-15% improvement over chain-ladder)
- Graph neural networks for fraud ring detection
- Parametric insurance with AI-triggered automatic payment
