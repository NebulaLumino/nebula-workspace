## Overview
Build an AI-powered customer LTV prediction tool. Users input customer purchase history (customer_id, purchase_date, revenue), and the app calculates historical LTV metrics, fits a simple predictive model, and generates segment-level LTV projections plus an AI interpretation of which customer segments are most valuable.

## Tech Stack
- Next.js 15 (App Router)
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS
- Recharts
- OpenAI SDK

## Features
- CSV upload: customer_id, purchase_date, revenue
- Historical LTV by customer (total, average, max)
- LTV cohort analysis by acquisition month
- Simple projection model: average order value × purchase frequency × average lifespan
- Customer segment breakdown (top 20%, middle 60%, bottom 20%)
- AI interpretation: "Top 20% of customers contribute 75% of revenue — focus retention efforts here"
- Visualization: LTV distribution histogram, cohort LTV curves
- Export: segment analysis as CSV, full report as Markdown
