**Status:** ✅ Complete

## App Concept
Generate brand manifestos and belief statements. Input: brand name, founding story, target audience, core beliefs about the world, what the brand is fighting for. Output: brand manifesto (500-800 words) with a belief-driven narrative, authentic voice, and emotional core that can guide all brand communications.
