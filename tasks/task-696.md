## App Concept

Generate high-open-rate email newsletter subject lines. Input: newsletter topic, target audience, email number in sequence, personalization tokens available (first name, company), urgency level, emoji preference. Output: 15 subject line variations with predicted open rate scores (high/medium/low), categorized by type (question, curiosity, fear of missing out, personalization, emoji-driven), preview text options, and A/B test pairing recommendations.
