## App Concept

Generate a tailored privacy policy for your website or app without a lawyer. Input: business name, website URL, data you collect (emails, payments, cookies, etc.), third-party services used (Google Analytics, Stripe, Mailchimp), and user rights you support. Output: a readable, GDPR/CCPA-aware privacy policy with plain-language summary and section-by-section explanation.

## Tech Stack

- Next.js 16 (App Router)
- Tailwind CSS
- DeepSeek API (OpenAI-compatible)
- TypeScript
