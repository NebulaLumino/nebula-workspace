## App Concept

Generate social proof notification campaigns (FOMO popups). Input: product/service, notification type (recent purchase, signup, review, viewing activity), notification frequency rules, copy style (urgent, friendly, authoritative), platform context (e-commerce, SaaS, lead gen). Output: social proof notification copy library with 10+ notification templates, trigger conditions, display frequency rules, smart delay recommendations, and A/B test variations. Includes "someone just purchased" and "X people are viewing this" templates.
