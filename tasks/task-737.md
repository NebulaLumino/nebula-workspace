## App Concept

Generate viral meme captions and templates. Input: meme format/ template name, topic/niche, tone (sarcastic, inspirational, relatable, commentary), target platform (Twitter, Instagram, Reddit). Output: 10 caption options with caption positioning (top/bottom), text style suggestions, and engagement prediction scores. Includes format-specific sizing advice.
