# Task 766: AI Investor Pitch Deck Generator

**Status:** ✅ Complete
**Type:** App Generator — Fundraising
**Priority:** HIGH

## App Concept

Generate investor pitch decks from business inputs. Input: company name, problem, solution, market size, business model, team, funding ask. Output: complete pitch deck outline with 10-12 slides (problem, solution, market, product, business model, traction, competition, team, financials, ask), speaker notes per slide, and storytelling tips.

## Tech Stack
- Next.js 16 (App Router), Tailwind CSS, DeepSeek API, TypeScript
- GitHub repo: `ai-investor-pitch-deck`
