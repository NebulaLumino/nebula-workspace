## App Concept

Generate optimized Contact Us pages that route inquiries correctly. Input: company name, support channels offered (email, phone, chat, help center), team locations/timezones, expected response times, department routing needs. Output: Contact Us page copy with department-specific routing (sales, support, press, partnerships), response time expectations, recommended form fields by inquiry type, FAQs for common questions, and office address/localization copy.
