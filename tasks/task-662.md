## App Concept

Generate YouTube/TikTok/Instagram video thumbnail concepts and copy. Input: video title, topic/niche, target platform (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram Reels), main hook or clickbait angle, brand colors/logo. Output: 3 thumbnail concept variations with headline text suggestions, visual element descriptions, color palette recommendations, emoji usage, and a text overlay guide. Includes tips for avoiding deceptive thumbnail practices.
