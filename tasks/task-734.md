## App Concept

Generate marketing slogans for campaigns and products. Input: campaign theme, product/service name, key message, target emotion, campaign duration (seasonal, evergreen), channel (social, TV, print, banner). Output: 15 slogan variations across formats (short punchy, longer story-driven, rhyme/alliteration, question-based), each with suggested font pairing and visual direction.
