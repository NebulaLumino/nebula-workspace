## App Concept

Generate compelling, SEO-optimized product descriptions from basic product details. Input: product name, category, key features, target audience, competitor products, tone (luxury, casual, technical). Output: multiple variant descriptions (short 50-word, medium 150-word, long 300-word), bullet point highlights, SEO keywords, meta description, and social media card copy. Perfect for e-commerce marketplaces, DTC brands, and catalogs.
