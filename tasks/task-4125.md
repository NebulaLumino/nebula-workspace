## Description
An AI-powered tool for individuals with body dysmorphic disorder (BDD). Helps users create personalized coping cards with evidence-based affirmations, reality-checking exercises, and grounding techniques. Users generate, save, and access cards during distressing moments.

## Technical
- **Framework:** Next.js 15 (App Router)
- **AI SDK:** OpenAI (DeepSeek compatible)
- **Deployment:** Vercel

## Workflow
1. User describes their specific appearance concerns and triggers
2. AI generates personalized coping cards with affirmations and reality checks
3. User edits and saves cards to a personal deck
4. Cards are accessible during moments of distress
5. Includes BDD psychoeducation and professional resource links
