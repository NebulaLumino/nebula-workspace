## App Concept

Generate legally-compliant email newsletter footers and unsubscribe pages. Input: company name, industry, email type (marketing, transactional, newsletter), geographic compliance needs (CAN-SPAM, GDPR, CASL). Output: footer text with unsubscribe link, physical address, privacy policy link, preference center copy, and legal disclaimers formatted for email clients.
