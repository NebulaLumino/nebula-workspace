## App Concept

Generate structured interview scorecards for consistent candidate evaluation. Input: job title, interview format (phone screen, panel, technical, behavioral), key competencies for the role, rating scale preference (1-5, thumbs up/down), interviewer name. Output: structured interview scorecard with candidate name/date header, competency-based questions with ideal answer guides, rating scale with definitions, notes section per question, overall recommendation (strong hire/hire/no hire/strong no hire), and structured comparison template for panel interviews.
