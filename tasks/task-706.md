## App Concept

Generate email sequences to convert free trial users to paid customers. Input: product type, trial length (7/14/30 days), key activation milestone ("first project created", "first report generated"), expansion upsell moments, plan upgrade path. Output: trial day-1 welcome email, milestone activation email at day 3/5/7, urgency email at day 12/27, upgrade CTA variants, and post-trial win-back sequence. Each with subject lines, send-time recommendations, and A/B test variants.
