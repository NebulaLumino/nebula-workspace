## App Concept

Generate detailed NPS survey results analysis and action plans. Input: NPS score (0-10 responses), number of responses, response distribution (promoters/passives/detractors), verbatim comments, survey time period, customer segments. Output: comprehensive NPS analysis report with score breakdown, theme extraction from comments, segment-wise NPS, competitor benchmark comparison, actionable recommendations for each detractor theme, and a closed-loop follow-up strategy for detractors.
