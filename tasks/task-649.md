## App Concept

Check content for brand voice consistency. Input: brand voice guidelines (or generate them), content to audit (text, copy, social posts, emails), consistency criteria (tone, vocabulary, messaging pillars). Output: consistency score with breakdown by dimension, flagged passages that deviate from brand voice, specific correction suggestions, overall alignment assessment, and improvement recommendations.
