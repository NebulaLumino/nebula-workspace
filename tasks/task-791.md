**Status:** ✅ Complete
**Type:** App Generator — Sales
**Priority:** HIGH

## App Concept
Generate cold call scripts for sales calls. Input: prospect persona, product/service, call objective (demo, discovery, objection handling), call stage (first 30sec, mid-call, close). Output: complete call script with openers, qualifying questions, objection responses, close techniques, and dead-end handling.
