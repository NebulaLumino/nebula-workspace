## App Concept

Generate customer health score reports and churn risk assessments. Input: customer name, product usage metrics (login frequency, feature adoption, API calls, team size), support ticket volume, contract details (MRR, renewal date, contract length), NPS score. Output: structured health score report with overall score (0-100), dimension breakdown (product adoption, engagement, support burden, commercial), churn risk rating (green/yellow/red), recommended CS actions, and expansion opportunity notes.
