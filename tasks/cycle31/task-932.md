## Research: AI Agents in the Metaverse & Virtual Worlds

### Overview
The "Metaverse" refers to persistent, shared, digital spaces — ranging from Roblox and Fortnite to Decentraland and VRChat. AI agents in these environments serve as inhabitants, creators, curators, and infrastructure.

### Key AI Agent Capabilities in Virtual Worlds

**1. AI-Driven World Building**
- Procedural terrain, city, and dungeon generation at scale
- AI agents that populate virtual worlds autonomously
- Dynamic event systems that respond to player behavior
- Tools: Promethean AI, Inworld AI, Modbox AI

**2. Intelligent NPCs as World Inhabitants**
- NPCs with memory of past interactions (permanent world state)
- Agents that form relationships, hold jobs, run businesses in virtual worlds
- Democratic systems where AI agents "vote" on world governance
- Examples: Altera's AI agents in Roblox, ServReality

**3. Content Moderation at Scale**
- Real-time monitoring of text, voice, and action in virtual spaces
- Context-aware moderation (cultural differences in emoji use, etc.)
- Proactive conflict resolution agents that intervene before escalation
- Challenge: 1000x more data per user vs. traditional social media

**4. AI as Virtual World Economists**
- Modeling supply/demand for virtual goods and services
- Detecting and preventing virtual world inflation/exploitation
- AI-driven dynamic pricing for virtual real estate and goods
- Virtual GDP measurement and economic health indicators

**5. Personalized Experience Engines**
- Real-time adaptation of world difficulty, density, social dynamics
- AI DJ that curates world music based on resident mood (via voice analysis)
- Automatic difficulty and narrative adjustment (like Netflix's bandersnatch)
