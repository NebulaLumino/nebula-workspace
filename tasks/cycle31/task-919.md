## Concept & Vision
A tool for worldbuilders, game designers, and virtual world architects to create deep, consistent fictional mythologies and lore systems. Generates creation myths, pantheons, historical timelines, cultural practices, and interconnections between world elements. Feels like having a mythology scholar design your entire world's spiritual and historical foundation.

## Specific Features
- World genre and tone (high fantasy/low fantasy/sci-fi/urban/cosmic horror)
- Pantheon design (monotheism/polytheism/animism/technology worship)
- Creation myth generation with symbolic motifs
- Historical timeline construction (golden age → collapse → rebirth)
- Cultural practices and rituals
- Prophecy and fate systems
- Relationship between gods/mythic beings and mortals
- Technological-magical balance framework
- Faction and civilization lore
- Archaeological mystery hooks (ruins, artifacts, forgotten history)

## Input Fields
- World genre and tone
- Scale (single city/continent/planet/multiverse)
- Existing lore elements to integrate
- Cultural inspiration sources (real-world cultures to draw from)
- Narrative purpose (backdrop/backstory/dynamic world)
- Mystery and intrigue level

## Output
Complete world lore package:
- Creation myth narrative
- Pantheon documentation (god names, domains, relationships)
- Historical timeline (5+ eras)
- Cultural practice guide (holidays, rituals, taboos)
- Prophecy system
- Faction lore sheets
- Archaeological mystery hooks
- Lore consistency guide for writers

## Tech Stack
Next.js 16, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, DeepSeek API, printable lore book format.
