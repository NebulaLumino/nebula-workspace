## Concept & Vision
A tool for game developers, virtual world designers, and digital creators to build rich, consistent avatar characters with complete backstories, personality profiles, visual specifications, and behavior trees. Generates NPCs, virtual companions, and digital human personas. Feels like having a character designer and writer team creating your avatar from scratch.

## Specific Features
- Avatar type (NPC companion/merchant/quest-giver/enemy/player-reset)
- Personality traits and speech patterns
- Visual appearance specification (age, build, style, distinguishing features)
- Backstory and motivation engine
- Dialogue style and vocabulary level
- Behavior decision tree
- Relationship to player and other characters
- Voice and animation style guidance
- Quest and content integration hooks
- Lore placement within the world's canon

## Input Fields
- Avatar role in the experience
- Setting genre (fantasy/sci-fi/modern/horror/ Slice of life)
- Character archetype (mentor/rival/ally/enigmatic/tragic)
- Visual style (realistic/stylized/cartoon/voxel)
- Age range and complexity of personality
- Story importance level (背景板/配角/主要角色)

## Output
Complete avatar character profile:
- Full character sheet with stats
- Personality and motivation breakdown
- Visual appearance description
- Backstory narrative
- Dialogue sample scripts (5-10 variations)
- Behavior decision tree (text format)
- Voice and animation guidance
- Lore integration notes
- Relationship map to other characters

## Tech Stack
Next.js 16, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, DeepSeek API, JSON export.
