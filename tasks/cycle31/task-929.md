## Concept & Vision
A tool for esports team managers, org founders, and competitive gaming organizations to craft compelling team profiles, player bios, and organizational narratives. Generates content for team websites, tournament registrations, sponsorship decks, and social media. Feels like having a sports PR professional write your entire team story.

## Specific Features
- Team profile with history, achievements, and culture
- Individual player bios with stats and backgrounds
- Coaching staff profiles
- Organization overview for sponsors
- Match result summary format
- Tournament history and results table
- Sponsorship opportunity deck sections
- Social media bio variations
- Match day lineup announcement templates
- Post-match interview question bank

## Input Fields
- Game(s) and region
- Team founding date and history
- Notable achievements and placements
- Roster (players + coaches)
- Organization type (amateur/professional/academy)
- Target audience (fans/sponsors/tournament admins)

## Output
Complete esports team package:
- Team overview narrative
- Individual player bios (3-5 per player)
- Coach/staff profiles
- Achievement timeline
- Tournament history table
- Sponsorship deck section
- Social media bio variations (Twitter, Instagram, TikTok)
- Lineup announcement template
- Post-match media kit structure

## Tech Stack
Next.js 16, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, DeepSeek API, HTML/text export.
