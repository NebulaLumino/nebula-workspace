## Concept & Vision
A tool for DAO contributors, governance leads, and decentralized org members to draft formal governance proposals. Generates well-structured proposals with rationale, risk analysis, budget breakdowns, and implementation timelines. Feels like having a governance expert help you navigate on-chain decision making.

## Specific Features
- Proposal type (treasury allocation/parameter change/rule amendment/partnership/roadmap)
- Structured template based on DAO best practices
- Risk and concern anticipation
- Budget and tokenomics impact analysis
- Community sentiment framing
- Snapshot vote simulation
- Forum post and Discord announcement generation
- Implementation checklist
- Voting deadline and quorum estimation

## Input Fields
- Proposal title and summary
- Type of governance action
- Treasury amounts and recipients (if applicable)
- Affected protocol parameters
- Expected outcome and success metrics
- Author reputation and credentials
- Timeline urgency

## Output
Complete governance proposal package:
- Forum-ready proposal post (formatted Markdown)
- Executive summary (150 words)
- Detailed rationale and evidence
- Risk assessment (3-5 major risks with mitigations)
- Budget breakdown (if applicable)
- Implementation timeline
- Community announcement copy
- Q&A preparation notes
- Voting recommendation talking points

## Tech Stack
Next.js 16, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, DeepSeek API, Markdown/GitHub gist export.
