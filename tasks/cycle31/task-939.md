## Research: AI Agents in Telepresence & Remote Presence

### Overview
Telepresence technology enables people to feel physically present in a remote location. AI agents extend this concept — acting as your representative in physical spaces when you cannot be there yourself, combining robotics, computer vision, natural language understanding, and autonomous navigation.

### Key AI Agent Capabilities in Telepresence

**1. Physical AI Representatives (Avatar Robots)**
- AI-controlled robots that serve as your physical presence in remote spaces
- Autonomous navigation in unfamiliar environments
- Real-time visual understanding of surroundings
- Natural language interaction with people in the physical space
- Face and voice recognition to identify attendees
- Examples: Double Robotics, OhmniLabs, Beam (Suitbots)

**2. AI Meeting Presence & Note-Taking**
- AI avatar that attends meetings on your behalf
- Real-time summarization and action item extraction
- Voice synthesis so the AI can "speak" with your vocal likeness
- Pose/gesture generation to match the speech rhythm
- Memory of past meetings and context to contribute relevant points
- Tools: Fireflies.ai, Otter.ai, Fathom, Recall

**3. Holographic Telepresence**
- Life-size holographic projection of remote participant
- AI-generated spatial audio (sound comes from where person is "standing")
- Eye contact and gaze awareness (AI adjusts your holographic position)
- Microsoft Mesh, Spatial, Arhtronica holographic stages
- Real-time AI dubbing into 40+ languages with lip sync

**4. AI Receptionist & Concierge Agents**
- Physical AI agent greeting visitors in lobby
- Natural language check-in process
- Security verification (ID scanning, face matching)
- Meeting room routing and scheduling
- Language translation in real-time
- Examples: Relay, Relay by Knightscope, DataCloud, ServReality

**5. Remote Expert Guidance**
- AI agent in a factory/facility that remote experts can control
- AR annotations overlaid on what the AI sees
- Real-time document scanning and knowledge retrieval
- Step-by-step guidance for maintenance/repair tasks
- Quality inspection with AI-assisted defect detection
- FieldAI, Upskill, Scope AR

### Technical Architecture

**Telepresence AI Stack:**
```
[Sensors: Cameras, Lidar, Microphones, Depth Sensors]
       ↓
[Perception: SLAM, Object Detection, Pose Estimation]
       ↓
[AI Brain: LLM + Vision Model + Audio Model]
       ↓
[Navigation: Path Planning, Obstacle Avoidance]
       ↓
[Actuation: Robot motors / Holographic display / Voice synthesis]
       ↓
[Connectivity: 5G / WiFi 7 / Edge compute]
```

### Challenges
1. **Latency** — remote control of physical robot must be real-time (<100ms)
2. **Bandwidth** — high-quality video streaming requires stable connections
3. **Navigation** — autonomous navigation in complex, dynamic environments
4. **Social acceptance** — people find telepresence robots unusual or unsettling
5. **Security** — remote-controlled physical presence raises safety concerns
6. **Battery life** — continuous operation limited by power constraints
