## Concept & Vision
A tool for Web3 project founders, community managers, and DAO operations leads to build thriving on-chain communities. Generates community structure, engagement programs, role systems, and growth roadmaps. Feels like having a community strategist who has built 10 successful DAOs.

## Specific Features
- Community structure design (Discord/server setup)
- Role and permission hierarchy
- Onboarding flow and welcome experience
- Content calendar for community channels
- Engagement programs (AMAs, challenges, raids, giveaways)
- Governance participation incentives
- Contributor pathway ( Lurker → Member → Contributor → Core Team)
- Moderation guidelines and bot setup
- Metrics and KPIs for community health
- Cross-platform strategy (Discord/Telegram/Twitter/Forum)

## Input Fields
- Project type (protocol/collection/dapp/DAO)
- Target community size (100/1K/10K/100K)
- Platform focus (Discord-primary/Telegram-mixed/Forum-heavy)
- Budget for community programs
- Team size for community management
- Timeline to launch

## Output
Complete community strategy:
- Server/channel structure diagram
- Role hierarchy with permissions
- Onboarding sequence (3-5 step welcome flow)
- 30-day launch content calendar
- Engagement program templates
- Contributor pathway description
- Moderation policy document
- Community health metrics dashboard
- Bot configuration recommendations
- Growth roadmap (Month 1-6)

## Tech Stack
Next.js 16, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, DeepSeek API, downloadable playbook.
