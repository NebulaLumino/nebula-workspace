## App Concept

Generate persistent follow-up email sequences. Input: original email subject/body summary, follow-up number (1st-5th follow-up), time elapsed since last contact, prospect engagement (opened, clicked, replied, no engagement), desired outcome. Output: 3 follow-up variations per follow-up number with subject line options, urgency level adjustment, break-up email variant, and reply-prompting strategies.
