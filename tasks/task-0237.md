### Key AI Capabilities in Sales

1. **Lead scoring**: ML models predict which leads convert
2. **Next best action**: AI recommends next step in deal
3. **Forecasting**: Predict revenue with deal stage + activity data
4. **Personalization at scale**: Customize every outreach email
5. **Objection detection**: Real-time coaching during calls
