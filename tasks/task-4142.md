## Description
Build an AI-powered DeFi yield farming risk analyzer. The app evaluates APY/APR across protocols (Aave, Compound, Yearn, Curve, Uniswap), assesses smart contract risk scores, impermanent loss calculations, token volatility risk, and generates personalized risk-adjusted recommendations.

## Requirements
- Multi-protocol APY/APR aggregation
- Smart contract risk scoring (Mythril, Slither integration)
- Impermanent loss calculator
- Token volatility analysis
- Liquidity risk assessment
- Portfolio exposure visualization
- AI-generated risk reports
- Telegram/Slack alerts for risk events
