## App Concept

Generate a Terms of Service document for your website, app, or marketplace. Input: business name, website URL, services offered, user obligations, payment/ refund policy, content moderation stance, limitation of liability preference. Output: a structured ToS with sections numbered, cross-linked table of contents, and a plain-English summary.

- DeepSeek API (OpenAI-compatible)
