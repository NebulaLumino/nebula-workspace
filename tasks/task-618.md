## App Concept

Generate influencer partnership contracts and agreement templates. Input: brand name, influencer name, platform, deliverable type (story, post, video, reel), compensation, usage rights, exclusivity period. Output: full contract document with terms, deliverable checklist, approval workflow, usage rights, FTC disclosure requirements, and IP ownership clauses.
