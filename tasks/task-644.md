## App Concept

Generate community guidelines for online platforms. Input: platform type (forum, Discord, social community, membership site), community focus/topic, target member demographics, moderation approach (self-moderated, light mod, strict), key values and behaviors to encourage, problematic behaviors to address. Output: comprehensive community guidelines covering welcome message, core values, participation rules, prohibited content/behaviors, enforcement tiers (warnings, mute, ban), appeal process, and moderation philosophy.
