## App Concept

Check if your business name is available across domains, social handles, and trademarks in one place. Input: your desired business name + optional keywords. Output: availability grid across .com, .io, .co domains + Twitter/X, Instagram, LinkedIn handles + USPTO trademark database, plus AI-suggested alternatives if taken.
