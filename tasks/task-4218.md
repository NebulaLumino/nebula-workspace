## Overview
Build an AI-powered lead scoring assistant. Users input a list of lead attributes (from CRM export), and the app generates a lead scoring model specification: which features matter most, scoring weights, tier classifications, and an AI-written interpretation of what drives high-quality leads.

## Tech Stack
- Next.js 15 (App Router)
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS
- Recharts
- OpenAI SDK

## Features
- CSV upload of lead data with outcome column (converted: yes/no)
- Or describe leads in natural language (e.g., "we have 10 fields: company size, industry, job title...")
- AI recommends top 5-8 predictive features based on signal keywords
- Generates: point-based scoring rubric (BANT-style or numeric)
- Lead tier classification: A (hot), B (warm), C (cold)
- AI interpretation: "Leads from companies with 50-200 employees convert at 3× the rate of smaller companies"
- Calibration notes: what score threshold to use for "ready to sell"
- Export scoring rubric as CSV, Markdown, or CRM importable format
