## App Concept

Generate structured podcast episode outlines. Input: episode title/topic, podcast format (solo, interview, panel), episode number and season, target episode length, guest name/bio if applicable, episode goal. Output: complete episode outline with cold open/cold read script, segment-by-segment breakdown with time stamps, talking point bullets per segment, interview questions if applicable, sponsored segment placeholder, and outro/call-to-action script.
