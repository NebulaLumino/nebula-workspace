## App Concept

Generate countdown timer page copy for limited-time offers. Input: offer type (sale, launch, event registration, early bird pricing), discount amount, deadline (specific date or "48 hours"), consequences of missing deadline. Output: full-page countdown copy with urgency headline, offer details, benefit-focused description, what happens when timer hits zero, and post-deadline fallback messaging. Multiple urgency intensity levels (moderate, high, extreme).
