## App Concept

Generate grant proposal narratives and supporting documents. Input: grant name and funder, organization mission, project description, funding amount requested, key outcomes and metrics, eligibility criteria met. Output: complete grant narrative with need statement, project description, evaluation plan, organizational capacity section, and budget narrative. Includes submission checklist and common grant writing mistakes to avoid.
