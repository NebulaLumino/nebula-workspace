## App Concept

Generate copy for interactive ROI calculators embedded in sales pages. Input: product/service type, primary metric being measured (time saved, revenue gained, cost reduced, efficiency improved), input fields to capture, industry benchmarks to reference. Output: calculator intro copy, field labels with helper text, result headline copy, results interpretation guide, and follow-up CTA copy for scheduling a demo or starting a trial.
