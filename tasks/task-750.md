## App Concept

Generate comprehensive business plans from brief inputs. Input: business idea, target market, product/service description, revenue model, competitive landscape, funding goal, team background. Output: complete business plan with executive summary, market analysis, product/service description, go-to-market strategy, operations plan, financial projections (3-year), and funding request section. Includes investor pitch angle.
