## Description
Build an AI-powered DAO governance proposal analyzer. The app tracks proposals from major DAOs (Uniswap, Aave, Compound, Nouns, Gitcoin), summarizes proposal content using AI, analyzes voting patterns, identifies conflict of interest, scores risk/reward impact, and sends alerts for proposals matching user-defined criteria.

## Requirements
- Multi-DAO proposal tracking
- AI proposal summarization (TL;DR generation)
- Voting pattern analysis
- Risk/impact scoring
- Delegate identity mapping
- Treasury allocation analysis
- Custom alert rules
- Vote tracking & history
