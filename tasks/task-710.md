## App Concept

Generate pre-launch "coming soon" pages that capture leads. Input: product name, launch date (or "coming soon"), teaser description, email capture goal, social proof elements (waitlist count, waitlist perk), sharing incentive. Output: compelling coming soon page with countdown or "launching in X days" copy, email signup form with compelling CTA, feature preview teaser, social sharing buttons, and post-signup confirmation copy. Multiple tone variations.
