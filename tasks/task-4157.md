## Description
Build an AI-powered crowdfunding campaign success predictor. Input campaign details (pitch, funding goal, rewards, creator history, category) and receive a success probability score, predicted funding amount, optimal funding goal suggestion, best launch timing, reward tier optimization, and AI-generated pitch improvement suggestions.

## Requirements
- Campaign profile analyzer
- AI success probability score (0-100%)
- Optimal funding goal calculator
- Reward tier pricing optimizer
- Launch timing advisor
- Pitch quality scorer
- Creator history analysis
- Category benchmark comparison
- AI pitch improvement suggestions
- Campaign analytics dashboard
