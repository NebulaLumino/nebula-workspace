## App Concept

Generate comprehensive customer onboarding checklists and sequences. Input: product type (SaaS, physical product, service), customer segment (individual, SMB, enterprise), onboarding format (self-serve, white-glove, hybrid), key milestones (setup, configuration, first value, expansion). Output: day-by-day or week-by-week onboarding checklist with task descriptions, estimated time per task, team responsible (CS, Sales, Engineering), milestone celebrations, and warning signs to watch for at each stage.
