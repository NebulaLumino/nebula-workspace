## App Concept

Generate a complete cold email outreach sequence in minutes. Input: prospect persona, your product/service, goal (demo request, reply, link click), number of follow-ups. Output: sequence of 3-7 emails, each with subject line variants, body copy (personalized, not generic), and send-time recommendations.
