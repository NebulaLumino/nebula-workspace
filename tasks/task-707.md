## App Concept

Generate abandoned cart email sequences for e-commerce stores. Input: store type, average order value, product categories, customer segments (new vs. returning), email series length (3/5/7 emails), discount offer strategy. Output: multi-email abandoned cart sequence with subject lines, product recall copy with product images placeholders, urgency and scarcity triggers, social proof inclusion, cross-sell/upsell recommendations, and optimal send-time sequence.
