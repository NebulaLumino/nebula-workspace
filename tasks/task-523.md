## App Concept

Recover lost sales with a timely, personalized abandoned cart email sequence. Input: cart value, product category, customer name placeholder, how many days since abandonment. Output: a 3-email sequence (Day 1 reminder, Day 3 incentive, Day 7 last-chance) with subject line options, body copy, and urgency cues.
