## Project
`ai-brand-voice`

## Description
Build an AI-powered brand voice tool that analyzes existing content to extract brand personality dimensions and generates a comprehensive brand voice framework with do/don't examples, tone guides, and messaging pillars.

## Requirements
- Content upload (website, documents, social posts, emails)
- AI brand personality analysis (Big Five, brand archetype mapping)
- Brand voice attributes definition (tone, vocabulary, style, rhythm)
- Do's and Don'ts examples auto-generated
- Per-channel tone adaptation (LinkedIn vs Twitter vs email vs website)
- Per-audience messaging (B2B buyer vs end consumer vs enterprise)
- Messaging pillars and value proposition framework
- Competitive brand voice comparison
- Content audit tool: score existing content against brand voice
- Voice guideline document export
