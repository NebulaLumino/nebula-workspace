## Research: AI Agents in Wedding & Event Planning

### Overview
Wedding and event planning is a $300B+ global industry that involves coordinating dozens of vendors, managing complex timelines, and navigating intense emotional stakes. AI agents are increasingly used to reduce the enormous stress of planning while enabling more creative and personalized events.

### Key AI Agent Capabilities in Wedding & Event Planning

**1. Comprehensive Wedding Planning**
- AI generating complete wedding timelines from guest count + budget + venue
- Vendor matching and recommendation based on style, budget, availability
- Budget allocation optimization across categories
- Seating chart optimization considering relationships, dietary needs, accessibility
- Guest list management with RSVP tracking and follow-ups
- Multi-event coordination (rehearsal dinner, ceremony, reception, brunch)
- Tools: Zola (AI wedding planning), A Practical Wedding, Joy

**2. Design & Creative Direction**
- AI mood board generation from couple's description
- Color palette and theme development
- Floral arrangement recommendations by season and venue
- Lighting design visualization
- Stationery and invitation design
- AI-generated "what-if" mood boards for venue decoration

**3. Day-Of Coordination & Logistics**
- AI-generated run-of-show for entire wedding day
- Vendor briefing documents and timeline distribution
- Real-time coordination during event (AI-assisted wedding coordinator)
- Contingency planning (weather backup, vendor no-show)
- Guest transportation coordination
- Emergency contact management

**4. Corporate & Social Event Planning**
- Corporate event concept generation
- Conference agenda optimization
- Team building activity recommendations
- Event marketing copy and invitations
- Sponsorship kit generation
- Speaker briefing document creation

**5. Budget & Vendor Management**
- AI contract review for vendor agreements
- Payment schedule management
- Vendor comparison analysis
- Insurance recommendation and documentation
- Post-event budget reconciliation

### Challenges
1. **Emotional stakes** — weddings are once-in-a-lifetime; errors feel catastrophic
2. **Vendor coordination** — independent contractors with no standardized systems
3. **Creativity vs. templating** — couples want unique, not generic
4. **Family dynamics** — AI must navigate complex interpersonal situations
5. **Budget sensitivity** — couples are often financially stressed during planning
6. **Cultural sensitivity** — wedding traditions vary enormously across cultures
