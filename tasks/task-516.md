## App Concept

Build a compelling ROI brief for any campaign, initiative, or investment. Input: initiative name, expected investment, target metrics (leads, sales, awareness), timeline. Output: structured brief with projected ROI range, cost-per-result estimate, key assumptions, risk factors, and a one-paragraph executive pitch to justify the spend.
