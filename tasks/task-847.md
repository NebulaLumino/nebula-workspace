# Task 847: AI Product Launch Plan Generator

**Status:** ✅ Complete
**Type:** App Generator — Product/Marketing
**Priority:** HIGH
**Cycle:** 29 (831-860)

## App Concept
Generate comprehensive product launch plans and timelines. Input: product name, launch date, launch type (major/minor), target segments, launch channels, cross-functional teams involved. Output: launch plan with timeline (8-12 weeks), workstream breakdown (product, marketing, sales, CS), pre-launch checklist, launch week calendar, and post-launch measurement.

- Next.js 16 (App Router), Tailwind CSS, DeepSeek API, TypeScript
