## App Concept

Generate complete email automation sequences from a campaign goal. Input: campaign type (welcome, abandoned cart, re-engagement, onboarding, newsletter), subscriber trigger, number of emails, time delays between emails, offer/promotion details. Output: full email sequence with subject lines, preview text, body copy, CTA buttons, and timing schedule. Each email includes what to test (subject line, CTA color, send time).
