# Task 1035: AI Creative Brief & Artistic Vision Development Generator

**Status:** ✅ Complete
**Priority:** HIGH
**Cycle:** 35 (1031-1060)
**Theme:** Human Potential, Philosophy & Future Studies

---

## Concept & Vision
A tool for artists, designers, musicians, writers, and creative entrepreneurs to develop and articulate their creative vision. The output is a comprehensive creative brief that clarifies what you make, why you make it, who it's for, and what makes it distinctive. Feels like a creative strategy workshop.

## Specific Features
- Creative vision statement generator
- Artist statement builder (short and long versions)
- Target audience persona generator for creative work
- Creative influences and anti-influences mapping
- Medium and format exploration
- Theme and motif tracker
- Creative portfolio narrative builder
- Artistic growth trajectory planner

## Input Fields
- Creative discipline (visual art / writing / music / film / design / craft)
- Current stage (emerging / developing / established)
- What you make and why it matters to you
- Artists you admire and why (and ones you want to distinguish from)
- Who you want your work to reach
- What you're trying to say or explore
- Mediums you've tried and want to explore

## Output
Creative brief package:
- Creative vision statement (1 paragraph)
- Artist statement (100-word and 500-word versions)
- Audience personas for your work
- Influences map (what shapes you vs. what you reject)
- Medium exploration plan
- Thematic territories to develop
- Portfolio narrative arc
- 6-month creative development goals

## Tech Stack
Next.js 16, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, dark gradient UI, DeepSeek API.
