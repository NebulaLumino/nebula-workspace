## Concept & Vision
A tool for therapy clients, life coaches, and individuals in transition to understand and reshape their life narrative. Based on narrative therapy principles (Michael White, David Epston), the app helps users identify dominant stories, find "unique outcomes," and write new chapters. Feels like a therapeutic workbook.

## Specific Features
- Life chapter segmentation tool
- Dominant story identification (what narrative are you living?)
- Unique outcome finder (times the dominant story wasn't true)
- Re-authoring exercise generator
- Values-in-action evidence collector
- Alternative story development
- Therapeutic letter writer (to yourself or others)
- Life document (legacy letter, ethical will) generator

## Input Fields
- Current life situation and transition
- Dominant narrative you tell yourself (e.g., "I'm not disciplined enough," "I always struggle with money")
- Recent times that contradicted this narrative
- Values that matter most to you
- Relationships central to your story
- Whether this is personal work or therapeutic with a professional

## Output
Narrative therapy package:
- Life chapter map (where you've been)
- Dominant story articulation with evidence
- Unique outcomes list (contradictions to the dominant story)
- Re-authored alternative story
- Values evidence log (proofs of your values in action)
- Letter to your future self
- Ethical will / legacy letter draft
- Next chapter outline (who you want to be)

## Tech Stack
Next.js 16, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, dark gradient UI, DeepSeek API.
