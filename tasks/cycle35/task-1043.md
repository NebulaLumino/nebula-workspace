## Concept & Vision
A tool for people who want to build sustainable wellness practices — not crash diets or unsustainable fitness regimens, but a genuinely personalized protocol that accounts for their life, preferences, and constraints. Feels like a functional medicine intake meets a wellness coach session.

## Specific Features
- Wellness pillar assessment (sleep, nutrition, movement, stress, social, purpose)
- Personalized protocol generator per pillar
- Energy mapping tool (when do you have the most energy?)
- Stress pattern analyzer
- Micro-wellness habit designer (things that take <5 minutes)
- Environmental optimization suggestions (sleep setup, workspace, etc.)
- Quarterly wellness check-in framework
- Practitioner referral guidance (when to see a professional)

## Input Fields
- Current wellness concerns (1-3 top issues)
- Current routines (sleep, meals, exercise)
- Energy patterns throughout the day
- Available time for wellness per day
- Constraints (medical conditions, injuries, budget, access)
- Stress triggers and current coping mechanisms
- Support system

## Output
Personalized wellness protocol:
- Wellness assessment summary
- Sleep optimization plan
- Nutrition guidance (not a diet — an eating framework)
- Movement prescription (what type, how much, when)
- Stress management toolkit
- Social wellness recommendations
- Micro-habits to add (<5 min each)
- Environmental adjustments
- Quarterly check-in framework
- Red flags that warrant professional care

## Tech Stack
Next.js 16, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, dark gradient UI, DeepSeek API.
