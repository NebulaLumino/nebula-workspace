# Task 1050: AI Philosophy of Life & Personal Ethics Framework Generator

**Status:** ✅ Complete
**Priority:** MEDIUM
**Cycle:** 35 (1031-1060)
**Theme:** Human Potential, Philosophy & Future Studies

---

## Concept & Vision
A tool for people who want to develop a coherent personal philosophy — a set of principles and values that guide difficult decisions, provide meaning, and create a sense of direction. The app draws from major philosophical traditions (Stoicism, Existentialism, Buddhism, Confucianism, Pragmatism, etc.) to help users build their own integrated worldview. Feels like a philosophy seminar adapted for personal use.

## Specific Features
- Philosophical tradition sampler (what resonates with you?)
- Core values extractor and articulator
- Virtue list generator (what character traits do you cultivate?)
- Decision-making principle builder
- Meaning-of-life framework generator
- Death contemplation guide (memento mori as motivation tool)
- Daily philosophy practice designer
- Personal ethical framework for hard choices
- Life philosophy document writer

## Input Fields
- Current worldview or religious/secular background
- What philosophical texts or ideas (if any) have resonated with you
- Central life questions you're wrestling with
- Ethical dilemmas you're currently facing
- What you want your life to stand for
- Any tradition or culture informing your values

## Output
Personal philosophy package:
- Philosophical tradition summary (top 2-3 that resonate)
- Core values articulated (5-7 with explanations)
- Virtue list (character traits to cultivate)
- Decision-making principles (3-5 rules for hard choices)
- Meaning framework (what makes a life worth living)
- Memento mori practice (death as a guide)
- Daily philosophy practice (5 minutes/day)
- Personal ethical code document
- Annotated life philosophy statement
- Quarterly philosophical reflection questions

## Tech Stack
Next.js 16, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, dark gradient UI, DeepSeek API.
