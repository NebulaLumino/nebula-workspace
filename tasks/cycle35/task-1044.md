## Concept & Vision
A tool for people in career transition — whether laid off, burned out, or simply ready for something new. The app generates a structured transition plan that accounts for financial realities, transferable skills, network building, and emotional phases of career change. Feels like having a career counselor who also understands your life situation.

## Specific Features
- Transferable skills analyzer
- Career values and culture fit assessment
- Transition timeline generator
- Financial runway calculator
- Identity transition support (who are you if not your job title?)
- Networking strategy builder
- Bridge role identifier (what you can do while transitioning)
- Gap narrative builder (for resume and interviews)
- Contingency planning

## Input Fields
- Current career situation (employed / laid off / wanting a change)
- Years of experience and field
- Skills inventory (what you're good at)
- What you want from the next chapter
- Financial situation (runway, needs)
- Risk tolerance
- Timeline urgency
- Non-negotiables (must have / deal-breakers)

## Output
Career transition package:
- Skills transferability analysis
- Career options map (related and stretch options)
- Financial runway analysis
- 12-month transition timeline
- Identity narrative ("the story of my career transition")
- Networking target list
- Bridge role recommendations
- Application strategy
- Interview narrative for career changers
- Contingency and fallback plans

## Tech Stack
Next.js 16, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, dark gradient UI, DeepSeek API.
