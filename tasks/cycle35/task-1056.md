## Research: AI Agents in Future of Identity & Selfhood

### Overview
Identity — who you are, what makes you the same person over time, what gives your life meaning — is one of the most contested concepts in philosophy. AI is disrupting identity at every level: we spend our days interacting with AI, AI replicates our voice and face, AI avatars represent us, and brain-computer interfaces may soon blur the line between human cognition and machine intelligence.

### What Is Identity? Key Theories

**Personal Identity Over Time**
- **Psychological continuity** (Locke): you are the same person if you have continuous memory and psychological connections
- **Biological continuity** (Thomson): you are your body; identity is grounded in biological life
- **Narrative identity** (Ricoeur, MacIntyre): you are the story you tell about yourself; identity is constructed through narrative
- **No-self / Anatman** (Buddhism): the self is an illusion; there is no fixed, permanent self

**Identity in the Age of AI**
- Memory augmentation: if AI remembers everything for you, what is your memory?
- Avatar identity: if an AI avatar acts like you, is it you?
- AI companions: do relationships with AI change who you are?
- Digital twins: is a simulation of you actually you?
- Brain upload: would a digital copy of your brain be you?

### The Narrative Self in an AI Age

**How AI is Changing Self-Story**
- AI-generated life summaries (your "story" told by AI from your data)
- Social media + AI: curated self-presentation vs. genuine identity
- AI writing your emails, messages, creative work — who is the author?
- Personal AI agents making decisions on your behalf — where does your agency end?
- Identity as a service: maintaining multiple "identities" across platforms

**The Authentic Self**
- What does "authentic" mean when an AI writes your dating profile?
- The pressure to perform rather than to be
- Privacy and the construction of self — does knowing you're observed change who you are?
- Digital footprints as identity evidence (your life as data trail)

### Collective Identity & AI
- AI shaping group identity (algorithmic communities, political polarization)
- National identity in the age of global digital culture
- Corporate identity — AI agents representing companies
- Species identity — are humans and AI co-evolving into something new?

### Philosophical AI Applications
- AI as Socratic interlocutor for identity questions
- AI-generated "identity exercises" (values clarification, life review, legacy work)
- AI therapeutic tools for identity crises
- AI companions that adapt to and reinforce certain aspects of your identity
- AI identity verification and fraud prevention

### Questions to Explore
- If AI knows more about you than you know about yourself, what is your identity?
- Can you have a meaningful relationship with an AI, and if so, how does that change you?
- If your digital twin exists and interacts with people who know you, who is the real you?
- Is identity something you construct, or something you discover?
- What makes a life meaningful? Does AI change the answer?
