## Concept & Vision
A tool for people pursuing financial independence / early retirement (FIRE). The app generates a personalized FIRE plan with savings targets, investment strategy, coast numbers, and optionality analysis. Feels like a thoughtful financial independence roadmap — not just numbers but a life design tool.

## Specific Features
- FIRE number calculator (25x annual expenses)
- Current financial snapshot analyzer
- Savings rate optimization recommendations
- Coast number calculator (at what point can you stop saving?)
- Withdrawal strategy analysis (4% rule vs. alternatives)
- Side income and business idea generator for FI acceleration
- Life design post-FI exploration
- Annual financial independence roadmap generator

## Input Fields
- Current age and target FIRE age
- Annual income and expenses
- Current savings and investments
- Asset allocation (if any)
- Housing situation (rent / own / mortgage amount)
- Passive income streams (current or potential)
- Risk tolerance
- What you want to do post-FI

## Output
FIRE planning package:
- Personalized FIRE number and target date
- Current savings gap analysis
- Monthly savings target
- Coast number and when you reach it
- Withdrawal rate analysis and sustainability
- Side income accelerator recommendations
- Post-FI life design sketch
- Annual milestones to FI
- Key decision points along the way

## Tech Stack
Next.js 16, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, dark gradient UI, DeepSeek API.
