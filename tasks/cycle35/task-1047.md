## Concept & Vision
A tool for aspiring entrepreneurs who have an idea (or several) and need to stress-test it before investing significant time and money. The app generates a structured validation plan with customer discovery steps, MVP design, and go/no-go criteria. Feels like a lean startup advisor.

## Specific Features
- Problem statement validator
- Solution assumption mapper
- Customer segment analyzer
- MVP scope designer (minimum and recommended)
- Customer discovery interview guide generator
- Go-to-market strategy sketch
- Financial viability first-pass
- Competitive landscape brief
- Pricing strategy framework
- Launch timeline generator

## Input Fields
- Business idea or problem you're solving
- Target customer description
- Your background and why you're suited to build this
- Capital available to invest
- Time available (side project vs. full-time)
- Risk tolerance
- Whether you have existing customers or audience

## Output
Business validation package:
- Validated problem statement
- Key assumptions and test plan for each
- Customer persona (refined)
- MVP scope (minimum viable and recommended)
- Customer discovery interview guide (5-7 questions)
- Competitor analysis brief
- Pricing strategy options
- Financial viability indicators
- 90-day validation roadmap
- Go/no-go decision criteria

## Tech Stack
Next.js 16, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, dark gradient UI, DeepSeek API.
