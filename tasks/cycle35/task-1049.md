# Task 1049: AI Volunteer & Social Impact Project Planner Generator

**Status:** ✅ Complete
**Priority:** MEDIUM
**Cycle:** 35 (1031-1060)
**Theme:** Human Potential, Philosophy & Future Studies

---

## Concept & Vision
A tool for people who want to make a meaningful social impact — whether through formal volunteering, starting a nonprofit, or embedding social purpose into their career. The app generates a personalized social impact strategy. Feels like a mission-driven social entrepreneurship workshop.

## Specific Features
- Social issue deep-dive generator (root causes, stakeholders, existing solutions)
- Impact type selector (direct service / advocacy / capacity building / research)
- Volunteer opportunity matcher
- Skill-based volunteering strategy
- Social enterprise model selector (nonprofit / B-Corp / for-profit with cause)
- Logic model generator (inputs → activities → outputs → outcomes)
- Theory of change builder
- Impact measurement framework
- Networking and partnership strategy

## Input Fields
- Social issue you care about most
- Skills and experience you can offer
- Time available
- Budget (for social enterprise options)
- Preferred impact modality (direct service / advocacy / research / building organizations)
- Whether you want to do this as volunteering or as a career
- Location (local / national / global)

## Output
Social impact plan:
- Issue analysis (root causes and key players)
- Impact type recommendation
- 3 volunteer opportunity recommendations (matched to skills + issue)
- Skill-based volunteering strategy
- Social enterprise model options (if applicable)
- Logic model (activities → outputs → outcomes)
- Theory of change narrative
- Impact measurement framework
- 6-month social impact roadmap
- Partnership and networking targets

## Tech Stack
Next.js 16, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, dark gradient UI, DeepSeek API.
