# Task 1032: AI Executive Coaching Session Generator

**Status:** ✅ Complete
**Priority:** HIGH
**Cycle:** 35 (1031-1060)
**Theme:** Human Potential, Philosophy & Future Studies

---

## Concept & Vision
A tool for executives, managers, and emerging leaders to prepare for high-stakes situations and develop their leadership presence. The output is a structured coaching preparation document with scenario practice, framing strategies, and reflection prompts. Feels like having a world-class executive coach on demand.

## Specific Features
- Difficult conversation script generator (feedback, termination, negotiation)
- Leadership presence assessment and development plan
- Stakeholder mapping and communication strategy
- Decision framing tool for complex choices
- Executive meeting preparation brief
- Board presentation coach
- Public speaking preparation and rehearsal prompts
- 360-degree feedback synthesis

## Input Fields
- Role and seniority level
- Upcoming situation (presentation / difficult conversation / negotiation / board meeting)
- Key stakeholders involved
- Desired outcome
- Specific concerns or fears
- Time available for preparation

## Output
Executive coaching package:
- Situation analysis and framing
- Talking points and key messages
- Anticipated questions and responses
- Worst-case scenario planning
- Post-session reflection questions
- Recommended follow-up actions

## Tech Stack
Next.js 16, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, dark gradient UI, DeepSeek API.
