# Task 1031: AI Personal Development Coach & Life Strategy Generator

**Status:** ✅ Complete
**Priority:** HIGH
**Cycle:** 35 (1031-1060)
**Theme:** Human Potential, Philosophy & Future Studies

---

## Concept & Vision
A tool for individuals seeking clarity on their life direction, career, and personal growth. The app acts as an empathetic but honest personal development coach — helping users articulate their values, identify strengths and blind spots, and create an actionable life strategy. Feels like a thoughtful mentor session, not a checklist.

## Specific Features
- Values clarification exercise generator
- Life quadrant analysis (work, relationships, health, meaning)
- Strengths and growth areas assessment
- Goal decomposition into 90-day action plans
- Obstacle identification and reframing
- Weekly review template generator
- Identity and values alignment checker
- Life strategy document builder

## Input Fields
- Current life situation description
- Age and life stage
- Key life domains of concern (career / relationships / health / creativity / purpose)
- Recent challenges or transitions
- What "success" means to the user
- Time horizon (1 year / 5 years / 10 years / lifetime)

## Output
Personal development package:
- Values hierarchy (top 5 core values)
- Life strategy narrative (2-3 pages)
- 90-day action plan with milestones
- Weekly review template
- Quarterly reflection prompts
- Accountability recommendations

## Tech Stack
Next.js 16, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, dark gradient UI, DeepSeek API.
