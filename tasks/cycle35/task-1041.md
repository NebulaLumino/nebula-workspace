## Concept & Vision
A tool for managers, mediators, HR professionals, and individuals navigating conflict. The app generates structured mediation frameworks and communication strategies based on the situation type. Feels like a conflict resolution handbook adapted to your specific situation.

## Specific Features
- Conflict type classifier (interest-based vs. rights-based vs. power-based)
- Mediation framework generator
- Interest identification tool (yours vs. theirs)
- BATNA (Best Alternative to Negotiated Agreement) analyzer
- Communication strategy builder
- Facilitated conversation agenda generator
- Agreement drafting assistant
- Follow-up and monitoring plan

## Input Fields
- Conflict type (interpersonal / team / organizational / partnership / family)
- Parties involved
- What the dispute is about
- Relationship history
- What each party wants vs. needs
- Power dynamics
- Previous resolution attempts

## Output
Conflict resolution package:
- Conflict type assessment
- Interest mapping (shared, compatible, conflicting)
- BATNA analysis for each party
- Recommended approach and strategy
- Facilitated conversation agenda
- Key messages for each party
- Draft agreement framework
- Follow-up and monitoring plan

## Tech Stack
Next.js 16, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, dark gradient UI, DeepSeek API.
