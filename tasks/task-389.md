# Task 389: Explore AI Agents in Governance & Democracy

**Status:** ⏳ Pending  
**Type:** Research — Curiosity  
**Priority:** LOW  

## Overview
The intersection of AI agents and democratic/governance systems — from policy-making to citizen engagement.

## Research Questions
- How can AI agents assist in policy-making? (analysis, simulation, public comment summarization)
- Government AI: chatbots for citizen services, algorithmic decision-making in benefits/police/courts
- Algorithmic accountability: who is responsible when an AI agent makes a bad decision?
- AI in elections: detection of misinformation, voter engagement, campaign optimization
- Participatory democracy: AI-facilitated citizen deliberation and idea generation
- Risks: bias amplification, surveillance, manipulation, democratic capture
- Current experiments: Estonia's e-governance, UK's Government Digital Service, Singapore's Smart Nation

## Key Technologies
- Deliberative democracy platforms (e.g., Consul, Decidim)
- AI for policy analysis (POLITICS AI, etc.)
- Government LLM initiatives
- Bias detection and fairness tools

Write research doc at `docs/ai-governance-democracy.md` covering opportunities, risks, current experiments, and governance frameworks needed.
