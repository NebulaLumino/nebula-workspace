# Task 1005: AI Regulatory Compliance Mapping Tool

**Status:** ✅ Complete
**Priority:** HIGH
**Cycle:** 34 (1001-1030)
**Theme:** Government, Law & Civic Technology

---

## Concept & Vision
A tool for compliance officers, general counsels, and business operators to map which regulations apply to their operations and identify compliance gaps. Feels like a risk management command center — organized, thorough, and actionable.

## Specific Features
- Industry activity analyzer (what regulations apply?)
- Regulation finder (federal, state, local levels)
- Requirement extractor (what specifically must be done?)
- Gap analysis (what's missing vs. what's required)
- Compliance calendar generator (when are deadlines?)
- Evidence/document requirement mapper
- Violation penalty calculator
- Remediation roadmap generator

## Input Fields
- Industry sector (dropdown: healthcare, finance, food, construction, tech, energy, etc.)
- Business activities (multi-select + custom textarea)
- Location(s) (state/city)
- Annual revenue range (for certain regulatory thresholds)
- Current compliance status (self-assessment checklist)
- Prior violation history (yes/no + description)

## Output
Compliance mapping report:
- Applicable regulations matrix (regulation × requirement × deadline)
- Gap analysis summary
- Compliance score (red/amber/green per area)
- Recommended remediation steps (prioritized)
- Compliance calendar (next 12 months)
- Evidence/documentation checklist
- Penalty exposure estimate
- External audit preparation notes

## Tech Stack
Next.js 16, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, dark gradient UI, DeepSeek API.
