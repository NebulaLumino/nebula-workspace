# Task 1008: AI Election Campaign Strategy & Platform Generator

**Status:** ✅ Complete
**Priority:** HIGH
**Cycle:** 34 (1001-1030)
**Theme:** Government, Law & Civic Technology

---

## Concept & Vision
A tool for political campaigns, candidates, and campaign managers to develop campaign strategy, policy platforms, and voter outreach plans. Feels like a professional political consultancy — data-informed, strategically framed, and operationally actionable.

## Specific Features
- Policy platform generator from candidate values and priorities
- Position statement generator (on major issues)
- Opponent comparison matrix (candidate vs. opponent positions)
- Voter persuasion message builder
- GOTV (get out the vote) strategy generator
- Campaign platform one-pager
- Debate prep brief (opponent vulnerabilities, counter-arguments)
- Fundraising email generator (from platform positions)
- Endorsement pitch letter generator

## Input Fields
- Candidate background (experience, accomplishments, biography)
- Office sought (federal / state / local, executive / legislative)
- District/state profile (demographics, political lean, key issues)
- Top 5 policy priorities
- Opponent information (if known — name, incumbent, positions)
- Target voters (base / swing / donor)
- Campaign tone (progressive / moderate / conservative / bipartisan)

## Output
Campaign strategy package:
- Position statements on 5 key issues
- Voter persuasion message guide
- Platform one-pager (voter-facing)
- GOTV strategy outline
- Debate prep brief
- Opponent contrast memo
- Fundraising email templates
- Endorsement pitch template

## Tech Stack
Next.js 16, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, dark gradient UI, DeepSeek API.
