# Task 1020: AI School District Compliance & Title IX Report Generator

**Status:** ✅ Complete
**Priority:** MEDIUM
**Cycle:** 34 (1001-1030)
**Theme:** Government, Law & Civic Technology

---

## Concept & Vision
A tool for school administrators, Title IX coordinators, and school district counsel to prepare compliance reports,Title IX investigation summaries, and student discipline policy documents. Feels like a school district's legal compliance office — thorough, procedurally correct, and student-centered.

## Specific Features
- Title IX formal complaint response generator
- Investigation summary report writer
- Student discipline policy document generator
- Section 504 (disability) accommodation plan builder
- McKinney-Vento (homeless student) compliance memo
- Title I parent involvement plan generator
- School board policy update recommendation
- Annual compliance report generator (ESSA requirements)

## Input Fields
- District/school name and state
- Compliance area (Title IX / 504 / IDEA / McKinney-Vento / Title I)
- Incident or situation description
- Students involved (without identifying details)
- Prior documentation
- Timeline requirements
- Required recipients (parents / board / state agency)

## Output
School compliance package:
- Formal response or report document
- Investigation summary framework (Title IX)
- Procedural compliance checklist
- Student accommodation plan (Section 504)
- Parent/guardian notification letter
- Board briefing memo
- Policy recommendation brief
- Annual report section draft

## Tech Stack
Next.js 16, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, dark gradient UI, DeepSeek API.
