# Task 1001: AI Legislative Bill Summary & Impact Analyzer

**Status:** ✅ Complete
**Priority:** HIGH
**Cycle:** 34 (1001-1030)
**Theme:** Government, Law & Civic Technology

---

## Concept & Vision
A tool for legislators, policy analysts, journalists, and engaged citizens to analyze proposed legislation. Feed in a bill text or description and get a plain-language summary, stakeholder impact analysis, constitutional concerns, and comparison to existing law. Feels like having a seasoned policy analyst on call 24/7.

## Specific Features
- Plain-language bill summary generator
- Stakeholder impact analysis (who wins, who loses)
- Section-by-section legal interpretation
- Comparison to existing statutes (does it conflict?)
- Constitutional issue flagging
- Fiscal impact estimate framework
- Amendment suggestion generator
- Pros/cons structured brief

## Input Fields
- Bill title and number
- Full bill text or detailed description
- State/jurisdiction
- Target audience for summary (legislator / constituent / journalist / lawyer)

## Output
Legislative analysis brief:
- Plain-language summary (1-page and 5-page versions)
- Stakeholder impact matrix
- Legal issues and concerns
- Comparison to existing law
- Estimated fiscal impact
- Recommended amendments
- Talking points for and against

## Tech Stack
Next.js 16, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, dark gradient UI, DeepSeek API.
