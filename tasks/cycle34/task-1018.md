# Task 1018: AI Immigration Court Defense & Asylum Application Generator

**Status:** ✅ Complete
**Priority:** MEDIUM
**Cycle:** 34 (1001-1030)
**Theme:** Government, Law & Civic Technology

---

## Concept & Vision
A tool for immigration attorneys, DOJ-accredited representatives, and detained immigrants to prepare court defense materials and asylum applications. Feels like a skilled immigration defense attorney's preparation system — meticulous, evidence-based, and humanizing.

## Specific Features
- Immigration court respondent brief generator
- Asylum application (I-589) preparer
- Withholding of removal argument builder
- Convention Against Torture (CAT) claim generator
- Credible fear interview preparation
- Bond hearing memorandum generator
- Voluntary departure request
- Cancellation of removal brief (permanent / nonpermanent resident)

## Input Fields
- Immigration court type (removal / asylum / withholding / bond)
- Respondent status (legal permanent resident / visa holder / without status)
- Grounds of removability
- Basis for relief (asylum / withholding / CAT / cancellation)
- Country of origin and conditions
- Supporting evidence available
- Prior immigration history

## Output
Immigration defense package:
- Court respondent brief
- Application for asylum and withholding (I-589 format)
- Supporting declaration (affidavit)
- Evidence organization guide
- Credible fear / reasonable fear interview prep
- Country conditions summary framework
- Legal argument citations
- Timeline and filing checklist

## Tech Stack
Next.js 16, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, dark gradient UI, DeepSeek API.
