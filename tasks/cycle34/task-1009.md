# Task 1009: AI International Trade Agreement Clause Analyzer

**Status:** ✅ Complete
**Priority:** HIGH
**Cycle:** 34 (1001-1030)
**Theme:** Government, Law & Civic Technology

---

## Concept & Vision
A tool for trade lawyers, government trade officials, and international business analysts to analyze trade agreement provisions and their implications. The output is a structured analysis of specific clauses — obligations, flexibilities, dispute mechanisms, and business implications.

## Specific Features
- Clause-by-clause legal interpretation
- Obligations extractor (what does this require of parties?)
- Flexibility provisions identification (exceptions, carve-outs)
- Dispute settlement mechanism explanation
- Domestic law interaction analysis
- Industry impact assessment (which sectors benefit/lose?)
- Compliance timeline generator
- Amendment proposal framework

## Input Fields
- Agreement name (USMCA, RCEP, CPTPP, EU FTA, etc.)
- Specific clause or article text (or topic area)
- Sector/industry of interest
- User role (government negotiator / trade lawyer / business analyst / researcher)
- Jurisdiction (which country's obligations matter most)

## Output
Trade clause analysis:
- Clause summary (plain language)
- Legal obligations identified
- Flexibilities and exceptions
- Dispute settlement process
- Interaction with domestic law
- Industry impact (winners and losers)
- Compliance requirements
- Negotiation considerations (if active negotiation)

## Tech Stack
Next.js 16, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, dark gradient UI, DeepSeek API.
