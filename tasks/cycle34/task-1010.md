# Task 1010: AI Diplomatic Communication & Foreign Policy Brief Generator

**Status:** ✅ Complete
**Priority:** HIGH
**Cycle:** 34 (1001-1030)
**Theme:** Government, Law & Civic Technology

---

## Concept & Vision
A tool for diplomats, foreign policy analysts, and government officials to draft diplomatic communications and policy briefs. Feels like a foreign ministry policy shop — precise, strategically framed, and aware of diplomatic protocol and precedent.

## Specific Features
- Diplomatic cable/letter generator
- Country situation brief generator
- Foreign policy speech excerpt generator
- Negotiation position paper
- Demarche generator (formal diplomatic representation)
- Summit briefing document
- Policy recommendation memo (to leadership)
- Press background brief (on-the-record talking points)

## Input Fields
- Diplomatic context (bilateral / multilateral, meeting type)
- Counterparty country/countries
- Issue area (trade / security / human rights / climate / etc.)
- Position to communicate
- Relationship context (ally / adversary / neutral, recent events)
- Audience (head of state / foreign minister / ambassador)
- Tone (formal / firm / conciliatory / urgent)

## Output
Diplomatic communication package:
- Formal communication (cable, letter, or demarche)
- Background brief (context, talking points)
- Anticipated responses and counter-arguments
- Recommended follow-up actions
- Historical context (similar past situations)
- Precedent analysis
- Risk assessment

## Tech Stack
Next.js 16, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, dark gradient UI, DeepSeek API.
