# Task 1006: AI Public Budget Analysis & Recommendation Report

**Status:** ✅ Complete
**Priority:** HIGH
**Cycle:** 34 (1001-1030)
**Theme:** Government, Law & Civic Technology

---

## Concept & Vision
A tool for government budget analysts, fiscal oversight officers, city council members, and journalism budget reporters to analyze public sector budgets. The output is a plain-language fiscal health assessment with spending analysis, department comparison, and fiscal stress indicators.

## Specific Features
- Department spending analyzer
- Year-over-year trend identification
- Per-capita spending calculator
- Revenue source diversification analysis
- Deficit/surplus projection
- Pension and OPEB liability flagging
- Service level vs. spending efficiency analysis
- Citizen guide generator (plain-language budget summary)

## Input Fields
- Government type (city / county / state / federal)
- Total budget amount
- Budget breakdown by department (textarea or structured input)
- Revenue sources breakdown
- Prior year(s) budget data (optional, for trend analysis)
- Population served (for per-capita calculations)
- Economic context (growing / stable / declining municipality)

## Output
Budget analysis report:
- Executive summary (fiscal health score)
- Revenue analysis and trends
- Expenditure analysis by department
- Year-over-year comparison
- Per-capita spending benchmarks
- Fiscal stress indicators
- Key concerns and risks
- Recommendations (prioritized)
- Citizen guide summary (plain language)

## Tech Stack
Next.js 16, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, dark gradient UI, DeepSeek API.
