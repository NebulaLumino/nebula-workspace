# Task 1017: AI Veterans Benefits Claim & Appeals Generator

**Status:** ✅ Complete
**Priority:** MEDIUM
**Cycle:** 34 (1001-1030)
**Theme:** Government, Law & Civic Technology

---

## Concept & Vision
A tool for veterans, Veterans Affairs (VA)-accredited claims agents, and Veteran Service Organizations (VSOs) to prepare VA disability claims and appeal documentation. Feels like a competent veterans' advocate's first draft — thorough, properly formatted, and legally supported.

## Specific Features
- Disability claim generator (VA Form 21-526EZ format)
- Secondary condition analyzer (what conditions are connected to primary?)
- Nexus letter framework generator
- DBQ (Disability Benefits Questionnaire) completion guide
- Notice of disagreement (NOD) generator
- Higher-level review request
- Board of Veterans' Appeals (BVA) brief generator
- Effective date maximization argument

## Input Fields
- Veteran demographics (branch, dates of service, discharge type)
- Service-connected conditions (with dates and descriptions)
- Current VA claims status
- Supporting medical evidence available
- Secondary conditions to claim
- Appeals status (NOD / HLR / BVA)
- Service treatment records summary
- Buddy statement information

## Output
VA claims package:
- Completed claim form (21-526EZ)
- Statement in support of claim
- Secondary condition analysis
- Nexus letter framework
- DBQ completion guide
- Appeals brief (NOD or BVA format)
- Effective date analysis
- Evidence checklist

## Tech Stack
Next.js 16, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, dark gradient UI, DeepSeek API.
