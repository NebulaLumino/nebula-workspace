## App Concept

Generate a comprehensive moderator playbook for community managers. Input: platform type, community size, moderation team size, common issue types (spam, harassment, misinformation, copyright, off-topic), escalation paths, response time requirements. Output: detailed moderator playbook with situation-specific response scripts, escalation decision trees, ban/mute reason templates, community tone guidance, positive engagement strategies, and shift coverage guidelines.
