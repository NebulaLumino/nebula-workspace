## Research Prompts
1. How is AI transforming financial risk modeling — credit risk, market risk, and operational risk; what models are used (XGBoost, deep learning, survival analysis)?
2. What is the state of AI in fraud detection — real-time transaction scoring, graph neural networks for fraud rings, and adversarial adaptation?
3. How is AI being used in algorithmic trading — from simple mean-reversion to reinforcement learning for portfolio optimization; what are the regulatory considerations?
4. What role does AI play in financial forecasting: earnings prediction, macroeconomic indicators, and alternative data (satellite, sentiment)?
5. What are the key risks and failure modes of AI in finance: model risk, flash crashes, bias in credit scoring, and regulatory scrutiny?
