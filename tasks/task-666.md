## App Concept

Generate partnership proposal one-pagers for strategic alliances. Input: your company name, partner company name, partnership type (technology, distribution, co-marketing, referral), mutual benefits, and specific terms. Output: professional one-page partnership proposal with executive summary, value proposition for both parties, partnership scope, go-to-market plan, revenue share or commercial terms, timeline and milestones, and key contacts.
