## App Concept

Generate viral-worthy headlines and article titles. Input: article topic, target platform (BuzzFeed, Medium, LinkedIn, news), content type (listicle, how-to, opinion, news), tone. Output: 20 headline variations with viral score predictions, emotional triggers identified, and SEO title alternatives.
