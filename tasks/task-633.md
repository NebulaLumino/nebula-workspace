## App Concept

Generate comprehensive FAQ pages from product/service descriptions. Input: product name, category, customer segments, common customer questions observed, support ticket themes, tone preference. Output: structured FAQ page with questions organized by category, concise answers (under 100 words each), expandable accordion UI structure, search-friendly formatting, and "Contact Support" CTAs where appropriate.
