## App Concept

Generate viral Twitter/X threads from a topic or piece of content. Input: topic or article URL, thread goal (education, entertainment, announcement, hot take), target audience, number of tweets desired, tone preference. Output: complete thread with hook tweet, each tweet numbered, threading format (↳ or numbered), engagement hooks per tweet, media suggestion per tweet, and reply-to-quote tweet strategy.
