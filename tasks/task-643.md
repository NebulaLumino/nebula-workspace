## App Concept

Define and articulate brand voice and tone guidelines. Input: company name, industry, target audience, core values, personality adjectives (friendly, authoritative, playful, etc.), competitor brands for reference. Output: comprehensive brand voice guide including core personality attributes, tone guidelines by context (social, email, support, blog), do's and don'ts, vocabulary recommendations (words to use vs. avoid), sentence structure preferences, and example copy for different channels.
