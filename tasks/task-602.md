## App Concept

Calculate and compare shipping rates across carriers. Input: package dimensions (weight, length, width, height), origin/destination zip/country, shipping speed preference, carrier preferences (UPS, FedEx, USPS, DHL). Output: side-by-side rate comparison table with delivery estimates, insurance options, fuel surcharge breakdown, and recommendations for cost vs. speed tradeoffs.
