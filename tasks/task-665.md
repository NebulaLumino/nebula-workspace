## App Concept

Generate quarterly product roadmap presentations for stakeholders. Input: product name, current quarter, planned features by quarter (Q1-Q4), strategic themes, customer requests ranking, technical constraints. Output: slide-by-slide roadmap presentation with timeline visualization (quarterly swimlanes), feature descriptions with business rationale, success metrics per feature, risk flags, and stakeholder talking points. Export format: structured markdown slides.
