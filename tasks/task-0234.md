**Key players:**
- Exscientia: AI-designed drugs entering trials

### Clinical Documentation (LLM Assistants)

- **Nuance DAX (Dragon Ambient Experience)**: Physicians speak, AI documents
- **Nabla AI**: Real-time clinical note generation
- **Amazon HealthScribe**: HIPAA-compliant medical transcription

### Privacy Considerations

- **HIPAA** (US): Patient data must be protected
- **GDPR** (EU): Very strict consent requirements
- **PHI** (Protected Health Information): Can't train on patient data without consent
- **De-identification**: Removing patient info from training data

### Key Insights

- AI assists, not replaces: final decisions always human
- Regulatory hurdle: FDA approval takes years
- Privacy-first: HIPAA compliance is non-negotiable
- Best current ROI: imaging diagnostics, documentation, drug discovery
