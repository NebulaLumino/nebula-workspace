**Status:** ✅ Complete

**Priority:** MEDIUM

## App Concept
Generate churn analysis reports with risk scoring. Input: customer list with tenure, MRR, product usage metrics, support ticket count, NPS score. Output: churn risk report with customer risk scores (1-10), churn signal flags per customer, cohort analysis, root cause hypotheses, and retention action recommendations by risk tier.
