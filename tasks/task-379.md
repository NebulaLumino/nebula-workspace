# Task 379: AI Partnership Agreement Summary Generator

**Status:** ⏳ Pending  
**Type:** App Generator — Business Development  
**Priority:** MEDIUM  

## Context
A plain-language summary of a partnership agreement for stakeholders who need the gist without reading the full legal doc.

## Spec
- **Input:** Partner name, partnership type, key terms (revenue share, exclusivity, IP, duration, termination), mutual benefits
- **Output:** Clear summary of partnership terms, obligations, and strategic value
- **Sections:** Partnership overview, key terms, mutual obligations, IP & confidentiality, duration & termination, strategic value

## Tech Stack
- Next.js 15 (App Router)
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS
- OpenAI SDK
