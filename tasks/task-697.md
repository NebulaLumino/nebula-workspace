## App Concept

Generate guest post pitches for blog and publication outreach. Input: your name, your expertise area, target publication/blog, article topic idea, their existing content themes, your portfolio links. Output: 3 pitch email variations (short/instant hook, medium/story-based, long/fully fleshed outline), subject lines, personalized opening paragraph, article outline pitch, author bio options, and follow-up email templates.
