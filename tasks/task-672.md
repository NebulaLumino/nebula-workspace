## App Concept

Generate structured sales discovery question sets for qualification calls. Input: prospect company, their industry, product/category being sold, deal size (SMB, mid-market, enterprise), number of stakeholders involved. Output: complete discovery call script with opening questions, needs assessment questions (budget, authority, timeline, need), pain identification questions, competitive positioning questions, and closing questions. Includes a framework selector (MEDDIC, BANT, Challenger Sale).
