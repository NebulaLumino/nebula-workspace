# Task 845: AI Employee Retention Strategy Generator

**Status:** ✅ Complete
**Type:** App Generator — HR/Management
**Priority:** MEDIUM

## App Concept
Generate retention strategies and action plans. Input: turnover rate, exit interview themes, team size, roles with highest turnover, compensation range. Output: retention strategy with root cause analysis, quick-win initiatives, long-term programs, manager training needs, and ROI of retention investment.
