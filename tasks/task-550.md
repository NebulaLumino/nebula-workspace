## App Concept

Generate customer health scorecards for CS teams. Input: customer name, product usage metrics, support ticket history, NPS score, renewal date, expansion signals, executive engagement. Output: color-coded health score (green/yellow/red), score breakdown by category, risk indicators, recommended plays (expand, retain, intervene), and conversation starters for QBR.

- Tailwind CSS
- DeepSeek API (OpenAI-compatible)
