## Description
Build an AI-powered Treasury bill and bond ladder builder. Analyze current Treasury yields across maturities (4-week to 30-year), construct optimal bond ladder strategies for income and liquidity goals, compare T-bills vs ETFs (TLT, TLT, SHY), calculate after-tax yield, and generate rebalancing alerts as bonds mature.

## Requirements
- Real-time Treasury yield curve
- T-Bill / T-Note / T-Bond price tracking
- Bond ladder simulator (1-10 year ladders)
- CD vs Treasury comparison
- After-tax yield calculator
- Interest rate sensitivity analysis (duration)
- AI ladder optimization for goals (income vs safety)
- Automatic ladder rebalancing reminders
- Portfolio stress testing
- PDF bond ladder report

## Tech Stack
