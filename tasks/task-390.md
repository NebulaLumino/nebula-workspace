# Task 390: Explore the Future of Human-AI Collaboration

**Status:** ⏳ Pending  
**Type:** Research — Curiosity  
**Priority:** LOW  

## Overview
The long-range view: how will humans and AI collaborate in 5, 10, 20 years? What does the future of human-AI teamwork look like?

## Research Questions
- The "centaur" model: IBM Deep Blue, Kasparov's centaur chess — humans + AI outperform either alone
- Current human-AI collaboration patterns: co-pilots, AI assistants, agents
- What tasks are better for humans? For AI? Where does the complementarity lie?
- Augmentation vs. automation: which is happening in different industries?
- New roles emerging: AI trainers, prompt engineers, AI ethicists, agent orchestrators
- The skills that remain irreplaceable: judgment, empathy, creativity, ethics
- Long-term: will AI be a tool, a colleague, or something else entirely?
- Historical parallels: the Industrial Revolution, computer revolution — adaptation and displacement

## Key Thinkers & Frameworks
- Erik Brynjolfsson: "The Second Machine Age"
- Jared Diamond: human adaptability
- DARPA's Human-Machine Symbiosis research
- The augmentation vs. automation spectrum

## Deliverable
Write research doc at `docs/human-ai-collaboration-future.md` covering the spectrum of human-AI collaboration, emerging patterns, and long-range outlook.
