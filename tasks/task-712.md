## App Concept

Generate compelling About Us pages that build trust and emotional connection. Input: company name, founding story, founders' backgrounds, company mission, team size, company milestones, values, awards recognition, location(s). Output: rich About Us page narrative with founder story section, mission statement, team culture description, company timeline/milestones, values breakdown, office location descriptions, and a "work with us" CTA. Multiple narrative styles (inspiring, humble, bold, quirky).
