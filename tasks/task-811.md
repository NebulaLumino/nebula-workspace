## App Concept
Generate A/B test hypotheses and experimental designs. Input: page/channel (landing page, email, ad), current metrics, problem being solved, traffic volume. Output: 5 test hypotheses with If/Then format, expected impact, statistical significance requirements, test duration estimate, and winner criteria.
