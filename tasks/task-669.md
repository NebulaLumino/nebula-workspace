## App Concept

Generate affiliate program partnership agreements. Input: your company, affiliate type (individual blogger, influencer, coupon site, loyalty program), commission structure (percentage, flat fee, tiered), cookie duration, payment terms (Net-30, threshold), brand guidelines compliance. Output: complete affiliate agreement with scope of license, commission terms, payment schedule, brand usage guidelines, prohibited promotional methods, termination rights, and liability clauses.
