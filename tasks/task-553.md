## App Concept

Predict and analyze customer churn risk signals. Input: customer name, product, MRR, usage frequency, last active date, support tickets, NPS response, contract end date, competitor mentions. Output: churn risk score (1-10) with confidence level, top 5 risk factors ranked, comparison to similar churned accounts, and suggested save plays with urgency level.
