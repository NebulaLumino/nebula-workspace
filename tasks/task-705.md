## App Concept

Generate pricing page copy that converts browsers to subscribers. Input: product name, number of tiers (2-4), tier names and prices, target audience segment, primary conversion goal, objection to overcome (price, value, fit). Output: complete pricing page copy including tier names/descriptions, feature lists with benefit-focused language, pricing callout copy, "most popular" tier reasoning, FAQ for each tier, money-back guarantee copy, and annual/monthly toggle messaging.
