## App Concept

Define and visualize KPIs from business context. Input: business type, team goals, current quarter objectives, available data sources. Output: structured KPI dashboard with top 8-12 KPIs, each with definition, target, current value placeholder, calculation method, owner, and update frequency. Includes chart type recommendations and insight prompts.
