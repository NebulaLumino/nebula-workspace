## App Concept

Generate professional email headers, banners, and email signatures for business communications. Input: name, title, company, contact details, brand colors, social links, preferred style (minimal, corporate, creative). Output: HTML email signature code, header/banner design description, social media icons suggestions, and formatting tips for email clients.
