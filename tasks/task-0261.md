## Research Summary

### What Are AI Voice Agents?
AI voice agents are intelligent systems that conduct real-time spoken conversations — answering calls, scheduling appointments, processing orders, and providing customer support — with human-like speech synthesis and comprehension. They go beyond text chatbots by handling the full phone call lifecycle.

### Key Platforms Explored

**VAPI (Voice API)**
- Developer-first platform for building voice AI agents
- Supports outbound and inbound calling
- Pre-built agent templates (sales, support, surveys)
- Easy integration via SDK (Python, Node.js)
- Competitive pricing with free tier
- Real-time voice with low latency

**Bland AI**
- Focuses on high-volume outbound calling
- Enterprise-grade reliability and compliance
- Neural voices with human-like intonation
- Inbound call handling with IVR
- Used for appointment reminders, surveys, collections
- Strong API-first approach

**Retell AI**
- Turnkey voice AI for phone interactions
- Conversational IVR and outbound agents
- Built-in CRM integrations
- Emphasis on natural conversation flow
- Slightly higher-level abstraction than VAPI/Bland

### Key Capabilities
1. **Real-time speech recognition** — Whisper-based or similar STT models
2. **LLM orchestration** — Decision-making, memory, tool use mid-call
3. **Voice activity detection (VAD)** — Knows when caller is done speaking
4. **Text-to-speech** — Neural voices with emotion and prosody
5. **Call pipeline** — VoIP/PSTN integration, recording, analytics

### Use Cases
- Inbound call routing and FAQ answering
- Outbound sales prospecting calls
- Appointment scheduling and reminders
- Customer support after-hours
- Debt collection outreach
- User research interviews
- Language tutoring

### Technical Architecture
- Speech-to-Text (Whisper, Deepgram, AssemblyAI)
- LLM for understanding and response generation
- Text-to-Speech (ElevenLabs, Cartesia, Azure TTS)
- Telephony (Twilio, Vonage, Asterisk)
- Conversation orchestration layer

### Pricing Models
- Per-minute pricing (most common)
- Per-call flat fees
- Monthly seat/agent subscriptions
- Free tiers for development

### Competitive Landscape
- Other players: ElevenLabs (Voice), Bland, Retell, VAPI, Calls.ai, PolyAI
- Differentiation: voice quality, latency, integrations, compliance (HIPAA, SOC2)

### Relevance to Workspace
Volcengine/ByteDance Seedream/Seedance are image/video. Voice agents are complementary for building full AI product suites. Could integrate with existing Next.js app fleet for voice-enabled versions.
