## App Concept

Generate new customer onboarding email sequences. Input: product type, onboarding milestones (account setup, first action, first value moment, expansion feature), number of emails in sequence, days to send each email. Output: day-by-day onboarding email sequence with subject lines, preview text, HTML body copy with inline CTA buttons, milestone celebration copy, and timing recommendations. Includes trigger logic (if they complete X, skip to day N).
