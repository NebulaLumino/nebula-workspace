# Task 374: AI Deal Desk Justification Generator

**Status:** ⏳ Pending  
**Type:** App Generator — Sales  
**Priority:** MEDIUM  

## Context
A structured justification document for deal desk / discount approvals, used by sales reps to get pricing exceptions approved.

## Spec
- **Input:** Deal details, customer requirements, competitor involved, standard price vs. proposed price, strategic value
- **Output:** Full justification memo with business case, competitive context, risk assessment, and approval recommendation
- **Sections:** Deal overview, strategic value, competitive factors, financial impact, risk mitigation, approval pathway

## Tech Stack
- Next.js 15 (App Router)
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS
- OpenAI SDK
