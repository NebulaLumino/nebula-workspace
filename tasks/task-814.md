# Task 814: AI Customer Segment Persona Generator

**Status:** ✅ Complete
**Type:** App Generator — Product/Marketing
**Priority:** HIGH
**Cycle:** 28 (791-820)

## App Concept
Generate detailed buyer personas from customer data. Input: customer segment name, common characteristics (demographics, firmographics, behaviors), purchase patterns, objections commonly raised. Output: detailed persona document with demographic profile, goals and motivations, pain points, buying journey stage, preferred channels, and message-to-persona mapping.
