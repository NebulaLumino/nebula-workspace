## Description
Build an AI-powered charitable tax optimization tool. Helps users maximize tax benefits through donor-advised funds (DAFs), qualified charitable distributions (QCDs), charitable remainder trusts (CRTs), and appreciated stock donations. Calculates optimal giving strategy based on income bracket, AGI history, and charitable goals.

## Requirements
- Tax bracket analyzer (federal + state)
- DAF contribution optimizer
- QCD eligibility checker (age 70½+)
- Appreciated stock vs cash donation calculator
- CRT benefit projection
- Bunching strategy optimizer
- Itemized vs standard deduction optimizer
- Charity recommendation engine
- AI tax strategy narrative
- PDF tax plan report
