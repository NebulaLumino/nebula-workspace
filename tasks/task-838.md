## App Concept
Generate 3-year financial projections from business model inputs. Input: revenue model (subscription, transactional, hybrid), pricing, customer acquisition cost, churn rate, team size, burn rate. Output: P&L projection, cash flow statement, headcount plan, key assumption documentation, and sensitivity analysis.
