## App Concept

Generate award nomination packages for industry awards and recognitions. Input: award name, category, eligibility criteria, your company's key achievements, notable metrics, customer testimonials, innovation claims. Output: complete award nomination package with narrative essay responses, supporting evidence checklist, press kit references, video script if required, and a scoring rubric self-assessment. Includes tips for each award category's specific judging criteria.
