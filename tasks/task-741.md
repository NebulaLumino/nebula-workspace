## App Concept

Generate personalized cold email sequences for outreach. Input: prospect name/company/industry, your product/service, outreach goal (demo, partnership, content collaboration), sender name, personalization hooks available. Output: 5 email variations with subject lines, opening hooks, body copy, value proposition, and CTAs. Includes spam score prediction and plain-text version for Gmail compatibility.
