## App Concept

Craft the perfect social media bio for any platform. Input: your name, profession, key achievements, personality traits, target platform (Twitter/LinkedIn/Instagram/TikTok), and tone preference. Output: 3-5 bio variations optimized for character limits, hook, and platform norms. One-click copy to clipboard.
