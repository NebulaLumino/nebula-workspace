# Task 841: AI Competitive Battle Card Generator

**Status:** ✅ Complete
**Type:** App Generator — Sales
**Priority:** HIGH
**Cycle:** 29 (831-860)

## App Concept
Generate competitive battle cards for sales teams. Input: competitor name, your product, their strengths, their weaknesses, price comparison, your differentiators. Output: battle card with competitor profile, their messaging, your counter-messaging, objection handling specific to this competitor, and recommended talk tracks.

## Tech Stack
- Next.js 16 (App Router), Tailwind CSS, DeepSeek API, TypeScript
- GitHub repo: `ai-competitive-battle-card`
