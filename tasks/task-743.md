## App Concept

Generate structured meeting agendas for productive meetings. Input: meeting type (team standup, 1:1, retrospective, brainstorms, board meeting), attendees, meeting goal, decision to be made, prep materials available. Output: complete meeting agenda with timing per item, discussion questions, pre-read materials, decision-making framework, and parking lot section. Includes virtual meeting facilitation tips.
