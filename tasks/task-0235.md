## Research Findings — AI in Education & EdTech 2026

### AI Tutoring

| System | Type | What It Does |
|---|---|---|
| **Khanmigo** | AI tutor | Personalized guidance, Socratic questioning |
| **Duolingo** | Language AI | Adaptive practice, error explanation |
| **Carnegie Learning** | Math AI | Cognitive tutor, real-time feedback |
| **Socratic by Google** | AI tutor | Explain concepts step by step |

### Automated Grading

- **Essays**: Turnitin AI, Grammarly, Edubampus — scores writing + detects AI
- **Math**: WeBWorK, automated partial credit
- **Code**: GitHub Copilot for feedback
- **Accuracy**: ~85-90% agreement with human graders at college level

### Adaptive Learning

- Platforms adjust difficulty based on student performance
- Spaced repetition (SuperMemo, Anki)
- Knowledge gap identification (where student struggles)

### Cheating Detection

- **AI-generated text detection**: 70-80% accuracy (imperfect)
- **Plagiarism detection**: Well-established, reliable
- **Proctoring AI**: Eye tracking, keystroke analysis

### Key Insights
- AI doesn't replace teachers — augments them
- Privacy concerns: COPPA (children's data)
- Best use: personalized practice, immediate feedback, administrative automation
