## App Concept

Generate comparison tables for products to help buyers decide. Input: 2-5 product names/SKUs, comparison criteria (price, features, specs, pros, cons, ratings), target comparison audience. Output: formatted comparison table (markdown + HTML-friendly), winner recommendations per use case, pros/cons summary, and buying guide tips.
