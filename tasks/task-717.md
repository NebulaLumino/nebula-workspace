## App Concept

Generate employee referral request messages that get results. Input: job title, key selling points of the role, referral reward amount, deadline for referrals, employee referrer's name, team culture snippet. Output: multiple referral message formats for different channels (Slack DM, email, team meeting), subject lines, message length variants (30-second read vs. detailed), easy-to-copy referral link CTA, and follow-up reminder templates.
