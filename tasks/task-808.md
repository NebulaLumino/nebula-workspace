# Task 808: AI Video Marketing Strategy Generator

**Status:** ✅ Complete
**Type:** App Generator — Marketing/Video
**Priority:** HIGH
**Cycle:** 28 (791-820)

## App Concept
Generate video marketing strategies and distribution plans. Input: product/service, video type (explainer, testimonial, demo, brand), target platform (YouTube, TikTok, IG Reels), budget tier, campaign duration. Output: video marketing strategy with content pillars, video topics per pillar, posting cadence, SEO strategy, and KPI framework.
