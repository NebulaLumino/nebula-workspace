## App Concept

Assess third-party vendor risks before onboarding. Input: vendor name, service type, data access level, integration method, region, certifications held. Output: risk score (1-10), risk breakdown by category (security, compliance, financial, reputational), recommended mitigations, and go/no-go recommendation with rationale.
