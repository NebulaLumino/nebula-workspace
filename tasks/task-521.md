## App Concept

Respond to customer reviews (positive or negative) in seconds without sounding robotic. Input: the review text, star rating, your business type, tone preference. Output: 3 contextually-aware response variants — warm and grateful for 5-star, empathetic and solutions-oriented for 1-3 star, with brand voice options.
