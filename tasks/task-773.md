## App Concept

Generate podcast guest pitches to get booked as a guest. Input: your expertise area, 3 target podcasts, episode topic idea, audience size, your credentials. Output: personalized pitch emails for each podcast with subject lines, talking point suggestions, and follow-up sequences.
