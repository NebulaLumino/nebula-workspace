## App Concept

Generate full email newsletters from topic prompts. Input: newsletter name and niche, issue number, main article topics (2-4), tone (curator, thought leader, casual, professional), length target, CTA goal (read article, reply, share, subscribe). Output: complete newsletter with subject line, preview text, introduction paragraph, article summaries with headers, links section, and closing CTA. Includes snippet/paste-friendly formatting.
