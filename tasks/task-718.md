## App Concept

Generate structured performance review summaries for managers. Input: employee name, role/title, review period, key accomplishments with metrics, areas for development, self-assessment summary, manager's overall rating. Output: complete performance review document with strengths narrative, areas for growth description, goal achievement summary, development plan recommendations, and overall rating justification. Includes 360-feedback synthesis if peer feedback is provided.
