## App Concept

Generate multi-touch testimonial request email sequences. Input: product/service, customer name, customer company, recent interaction or milestone achieved, platform to review (Google, G2, Capterra, Trustpilot, internal). Output: 3-email testimonial request sequence with subject lines, email timing recommendations, personalized opening referencing their success, specific question prompts for rich testimonials, and direct link to review platform. Includes escalation path for non-responders.
