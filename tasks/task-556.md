## App Concept

Generate cohort analysis reports from behavioral data. Input: product type, user acquisition month, retention periods, revenue per cohort, churn by period, segment breakdown. Output: cohort analysis report with retention curves, revenue retention by cohort, comparative analysis between cohorts, and actionable insights on what's driving retention differences.
