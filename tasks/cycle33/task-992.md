## Research: AI Agents in Emotional Design & Affective Computing Products

### Overview
Affective computing is the study and development of systems that can recognize, interpret, process, and simulate human emotional states. Combined with AI agents, this creates products that don't just respond to what you say — they respond to how you feel. This is one of the most commercially promising and ethically complex AI domains.

### Key AI Agent Capabilities in Emotional Design

**1. Emotion Recognition & Response**
- Real-time voice tone analysis (is the user frustrated, confused, bored?)
- Facial expression recognition (via camera — with significant privacy implications)
- Text sentiment and emotional subtext detection
- Physiological signal interpretation (heart rate, galvanic skin response — wearables)
- Multi-modal emotion fusion (combining voice + text + face + biometrics)

**2. Adaptive AI Companions**
- AI that adjusts tone based on detected user emotion (gentler when frustrated)
- Mental wellness check-ins: AI detects low mood and offers support
- Grief and loss support AI companions
- Social skills training AI (autism therapy, social anxiety)
- AI romantic relationship coach

**3. Emotionally Intelligent Customer Service**
- AI agents that detect customer anger before escalation
- Tone-adaptive responses (matching or calming aggressive customers)
- Emotion-aware routing (angry customers → senior agents, immediately)
- Post-interaction sentiment tracking for quality assurance

**4. Affective Product Design Tools**
- AI tools that generate designs evoking specific emotional responses
- Emotional branding: AI analyzes brand personality and suggests design systems
- UX copy that matches user emotional state
- A/B testing emotional response to designs (eye tracking, facial coding)

**5. Empathetic AI Health Applications**
- AI therapy tools (Woebot, Wysa) for CBT
- AI companion for elderly (detects loneliness, initiates conversation)
- Pain assessment AI (for clinical settings where patients can't verbalize)
- AI childbirth support (emotional coaching through labor)

### Technical Stack
```
[Input: Voice / Text / Video / Biometrics]
       ↓
[Feature Extraction: prosody, spectral, facial landmarks]
       ↓
[Emotion Classification: CNN/RNN/Transformer on labeled emotion datasets]
       ↓
[Context Integration: LLM adds nuance beyond basic emotion labels]
       ↓
[Adaptive Response: LLM generates emotionally appropriate output]
```

**Key Datasets:**
- IEMOCAP (actress-acted emotional speech)
- RAVDESS (audio-visual emotional speech)
- FER (facial expression)
- EmoContext (conversational emotion)

### Ethical Considerations
1. **Privacy** — continuous emotion surveillance is deeply invasive
2. **Manipulation** — emotion-aware AI could exploit emotional vulnerabilities
3. **Accuracy disparities** — emotion detection less accurate for non-Western faces/expressions
4. **Consent** — do users know they're being analyzed emotionally?
5. **Bias** — mental health AI performing differently across demographics
6. **Replacement** — AI emotional support vs. human connection

### Future Trajectory (2026-2030)
- Emotion-aware AI assistants that know when you're stressed before you do
- AI relationship coaches that detect tension in couples' conversations
- Empathetic AI teachers that adjust pacing based on student frustration
- "Emotional privacy" as a legal right (GDPR extension)
- AI animals/pets with genuine-seeming emotional responses for therapeutic use
