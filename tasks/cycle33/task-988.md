## Concept & Vision
A tool for public health officials, NGO communications teams, and healthcare marketers to develop complete health campaign strategies and materials. The output follows CDC/WHO social marketing frameworks — evidence-based, behaviorally targeted, and culturally appropriate.

## Specific Features
- Health campaign strategy generator (based on CDCynergy model)
- Target audience persona builder (demographic, psychographic, behavioral)
- Key message generator (3-5 core messages per audience)
- Material ideas generator (posters, flyers, social, video scripts)
- Behavioral change theory application (Health Belief Model, Theory of Planned Behavior)
- Channel strategy (where to reach the audience)
- Evaluation metrics framework
- Crisis communications plan for health emergencies

## Input Fields
- Health topic (e.g., vaccination, diabetes prevention, opioid awareness, STI testing)
- Target population (age, geography, cultural background)
- Campaign objective (awareness / behavior change / screening uptake / vaccine confidence)
- Budget tier (small community / state-level / national)
- Primary channels (social media / TV / community outreach / healthcare settings)
- Key behaviors to promote
- Competing messages / misinformation to address

## Output
Complete health campaign package:
- Situational analysis
- Target audience personas
- Behavioral objectives (SMART)
- Key messages
- Channel strategy
- Material concepts (with copy outlines)
- Evaluation plan and KPIs
- Timeline and budget estimate

## AI Model
DeepSeek via `/api/generate`.

## Tech Stack
Next.js 16, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, dark gradient UI.
